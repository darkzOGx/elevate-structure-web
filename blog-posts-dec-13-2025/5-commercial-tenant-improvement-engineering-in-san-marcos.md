## What Are Common TI Engineering Challenges in San Marcos?

**Direct Answer:** Common challenges include lack of original building drawings, hidden structural conditions, limitations of existing structures, and coordination with architectural vision. Many existing commercial buildings have limited structural documentation, requiring field investigation.

**Challenge 1: Missing Building Plans**

**Problem:** Many existing commercial buildings lack original structural drawings, making modification analysis difficult.

**Solution:** Field investigation to document existing conditions, including exploratory openings if needed to verify concealed framing and connections.

**Challenge 2: Load-Bearing Wall Identification**

**Problem:** Tenants often want to remove walls without knowing which are load-bearing.

**Solution:** Structural analysis to identify load-bearing elements, then design solutions that achieve desired openings while maintaining structural integrity.

**Challenge 3: Mezzanine Load Limitations**

**Problem:** Existing buildings may not have capacity for desired mezzanine loads.

**Solution:** Creative engineering solutions including column additions, load distribution systems, or alternative mezzanine configurations that work within existing structural limitations.
