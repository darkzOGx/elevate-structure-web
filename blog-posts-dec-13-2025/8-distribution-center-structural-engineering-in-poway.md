## What Are Common Distribution Center Engineering Challenges in Poway?

**Direct Answer:** Common challenges include high-bay clear span requirements, seismic rack anchorage, fire code coordination for high-pile storage, and accommodating automated material handling systems.

**Challenge 1: High-Bay Construction**

**Problem:** Distribution centers require maximum clear heights (40+ feet) with large clear spans for logistics efficiency, challenging structural economy.

**Solution:** Optimized structural systems using tilt-up or steel construction to achieve required heights and spans while minimizing construction costs.

**Challenge 2: Rack Anchorage Compliance**

**Problem:** High-pile storage racks must be anchored to resist seismic forces per California codes, requiring engineering documentation.

**Solution:** Comprehensive rack anchorage engineering per ANSI/RMI MH16.1, including seismic analysis, anchor design, and coordination with fire department for storage permits.
