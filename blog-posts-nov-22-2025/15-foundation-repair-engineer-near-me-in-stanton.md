## Signs You Need a Foundation Repair Engineer Near Stanton

**Visible Foundation Cracks**

Cracks indicating potential foundation problems:
- Cracks wider than 1/4 inch in foundation concrete
- Diagonal cracks at corners or openings
- Horizontal cracks in foundation walls
- Cracks that have widened over time
- Multiple crack patterns throughout foundation

Not all foundation cracks are serious, but professional evaluation determines which cracks indicate structural problems requiring repair.

**Interior Warning Signs**

Inside your Stanton home, watch for:
- Floor slopes or unevenness (marbles rolling, water pooling)
- Cracks in drywall, especially at corners or above doors/windows
- Doors and windows that stick or won't close properly
- Gaps between walls and ceiling or walls and floor
- Tile cracks or "tenting" (lifting from floor)
- Cabinets separating from walls

These symptoms often indicate foundation settlement or movement requiring engineering evaluation.

**Exterior Warning Signs**

Outside your property, look for:
- Stucco cracks, particularly diagonal or stair-step patterns
- Brick veneer cracks following mortar joints
- Gaps between foundation and structure
- Chimney leaning or separating from house
- Foundation visible above ground (exposed due to settlement)
- Poor drainage with water pooling near foundation

**Before Property Purchase**

When buying property in Stanton:
- Any visible foundation concerns during property showing
- Seller disclosure mentioning foundation repairs or problems
- Property built 1950s-1970s (higher risk period)
- Properties on expansive soil areas (most of Stanton)
- Recommendation from home inspector to get engineering evaluation

Pre-purchase foundation engineering evaluation costs $750-$2,000 but can save tens of thousands by identifying problems before purchase or negotiating appropriate price adjustments.
