## What Are Key Structural Considerations for Auto Service Facilities?

**Direct Answer:** Key considerations include floor slab design for vehicle and lift loads, clear height for lift operations, overhead door structural support, equipment anchorage, and Anaheim building department requirements for automotive occupancies.

**Floor Slab Design**
Auto service floors must support vehicle weight plus dynamic loads from lift operation. Existing buildings require capacity evaluation before lift installation.

**Seismic Anchorage**
California requires seismic anchorage for vehicle lifts, overhead equipment, and storage systems. Proper anchorage prevents equipment failure during earthquakes.

**Environmental Separation**
Some auto service operations require separation from adjacent spaces. Structural design coordinates with fire separation and environmental control requirements.
