## What Is Pre-Engineered Metal Building Engineering?

Pre-engineered metal building engineering bridges the gap between manufacturer-provided building packages and site-specific requirements. While PEMB manufacturers provide framing design for standardized load cases, California projects require licensed Professional Engineer design for foundations, seismic adaptations, and local code compliance.

Murrieta's business growth and cost-effective land make PEMBs popular for industrial, commercial, and agricultural applications. Our engineers ensure these efficient building systems meet California requirements.

## What PEMB Projects Require Engineering in Murrieta?

**Direct Answer:** PEMB projects requiring engineering include foundation design for any metal building, seismic connection engineering, California Building Code compliance review, building additions to existing PEMBs, and mezzanine or crane installations within metal buildings.

Standard PEMB designs may not fully address California seismic requirements. We verify lateral system adequacy and design any required enhancements.

**Building Additions**
