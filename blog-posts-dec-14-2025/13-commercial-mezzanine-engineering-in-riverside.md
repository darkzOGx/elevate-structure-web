## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is the maximum mezzanine size allowed?**
California Building Code generally limits mezzanine area to 1/3 of the floor below. Larger mezzanines may be possible with building reclassification.

**Do mezzanines require fire sprinklers?**
Sprinkler requirements depend on building classification, mezzanine use, and fire separation. We identify applicable requirements during design.

**How long does Riverside permit approval take?**
Riverside Building & Safety typically completes plan check in 3-5 weeks for mezzanine projects.

**Can mezzanines be built in leased buildings?**
Yes, with landlord approval. We coordinate with property owners and provide documentation satisfying lease requirements.
