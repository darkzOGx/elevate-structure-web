## What Commercial Buildings Require Seismic Retrofit in Glendale?

**Direct Answer:** Buildings requiring seismic retrofit include soft-story multi-family buildings (per LA County requirements), unreinforced masonry (URM) structures, non-ductile concrete buildings, and any building undergoing substantial renovation that triggers code upgrade requirements.

**Soft-Story Buildings**
Multi-story buildings with weak ground floors (parking, retail, large openings) require seismic strengthening. Glendale follows Los Angeles County retrofit requirements for soft-story buildings.

**Unreinforced Masonry (URM) Buildings**
Older brick buildings without steel or concrete reinforcement face collapse risk in earthquakes. URM retrofit is mandatory in many jurisdictions and recommended in all.

**Non-Ductile Concrete Buildings**
Pre-1976 concrete buildings often lack ductile detailing required for seismic performance. Retrofit addresses column, beam, and joint deficiencies.

**Tilt-Up Buildings**
Pre-1994 tilt-up concrete buildings may have inadequate panel-to-roof connections requiring retrofit.

## What Are Common Retrofit Solutions for Glendale Buildings?

**Direct Answer:** Common solutions include steel moment frames for soft-story conditions, plywood sheathing for wood-frame strengthening, steel strong-backs for URM walls, concrete jacketing for columns, and fiber-reinforced polymer (FRP) wrapping for targeted strengthening.

**Steel Moment Frames**
Adding steel frames in soft-story openings provides lateral resistance while maintaining parking and retail access. Most common solution for soft-story apartments.

**Plywood Sheathing**
Adding structural plywood to existing walls increases lateral capacity cost-effectively. Often combined with foundation anchorage.

**URM Strengthening**
Bracing or replacing unreinforced masonry with steel strong-backs, through-bolts, and parapet bracing reduces collapse risk.

**Concrete Column Jacketing**
Wrapping deficient concrete columns with steel or concrete jackets improves ductility and shear capacity.
