## What Restaurant Projects Require Structural Engineering in Costa Mesa?

**Direct Answer:** Restaurant projects requiring structural engineering include commercial kitchen installations, walk-in cooler/freezer additions, hood system support, bar construction, mezzanine dining areas, outdoor patio structures, and any modification affecting building structural systems.

**Commercial Kitchen Equipment**
Heavy commercial cooking equipment including ranges, ovens, steam kettles, and dishwashers require floor capacity verification and equipment anchorage design. Walk-in coolers and freezers need structural support analysis.

**Hood & Exhaust Systems**
Commercial kitchen hood systems require roof penetrations, structural support for equipment weight, and coordination with rooftop units. Our engineers design appropriate structural modifications.

**Bar & Service Counter Construction**
Built-in bars with heavy equipment, ice machines, and glass storage require structural evaluation and support design. Under-bar refrigeration adds concentrated floor loads.

**Outdoor Dining Structures**
Costa Mesa's climate supports year-round outdoor dining. Permanent patio structures, shade canopies, and outdoor kitchen areas require structural engineering for wind, seismic, and occupancy loads.
