## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do I need a structural engineer for hillside construction in Laguna Beach?**
Yes, California law requires a licensed PE for hillside foundation and retaining wall design. Laguna Beach Building Department requires PE-stamped plans for all structural hillside work.

**How much does hillside engineering cost in Laguna Beach?**
Hillside engineering typically costs $8,000-$45,000 depending on project scope. Retaining walls average $6,000-$15,000, while complete hillside homes range $25,000-$60,000.

**What is a caisson foundation?**
A caisson (or drilled pier) is a deep concrete foundation that extends through unstable surface soils to reach stable bedrock. Caissons are common in Laguna Beach hillside construction.

**Do I need a Coastal Commission permit in Laguna Beach?**
Properties in the Coastal Zone (most of Laguna Beach) require Coastal Development Permits for new construction and significant modifications. We provide CDP application support.

**How long does hillside permit approval take in Laguna Beach?**
City permits typically require 4-8 weeks. Projects requiring Coastal Commission approval may take 6-12 months or longer.
