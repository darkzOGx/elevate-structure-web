## What Is Industrial Warehouse Engineering?

**Direct Answer:**
Industrial warehouse engineering encompasses structural design for distribution and manufacturing facilities, including building structures, rack anchorage systems, mezzanines, loading docks, and heavy equipment foundations. These projects require analysis of floor load capacity, seismic bracing for tall storage systems, and clear-span roof structures supporting operational efficiency.

Warehouse engineering addresses:

- **Floor Load Capacity**: Supporting rack systems and equipment
- **Rack Anchorage**: Seismic bracing for storage systems
- **Mezzanines**: Adding usable floor area
- **Loading Docks**: Truck access and equipment
- **Equipment Foundations**: Conveyors, machinery, robots
