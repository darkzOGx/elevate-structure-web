## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do I need a structural engineer for a home addition in Brentwood?**
Yes, California requires licensed PE design for structural additions. LA Building & Safety requires PE-stamped plans for permit approval.

**Can my existing foundation support a second story?**
Many foundations can support additional levels with strengthening. We evaluate existing conditions and design upgrades if needed.

**How long does addition engineering take?**
Most addition engineering requires 4-8 weeks. Complex projects may require 8-12 weeks.

**Does the addition need to match my existing house?**
Structurally, additions connect to existing homes. Architectural matching is a design decision, but structural connections are always required.
