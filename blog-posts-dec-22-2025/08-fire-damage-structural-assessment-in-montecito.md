## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do I need a structural engineer after fire damage?**
Yes, professional structural assessment is essential for safety determination, insurance documentation, and reconstruction planning. Many insurance companies require PE reports for major claims.

**How soon can you assess fire damage?**
We can respond within 24-48 hours after site access is permitted by fire and building officials. Emergency assessments for safety determinations can often be expedited.

**Does insurance cover structural assessment costs?**
Yes, most homeowner's policies cover professional assessment costs as part of the claims process. Keep all receipts for reimbursement.

**What is the difference between repair and replacement?**
Repair addresses specific damage while retaining the existing structure. Replacement involves demolition and reconstruction. The determination depends on damage extent, cost comparison, and code requirements.

**Do I have to rebuild to current codes?**
Yes, when repair costs exceed 50% of building value, structures must be brought up to current building codes. This often includes fire-resistant construction requirements under Chapter 7A.
