## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do I need a structural engineer for oceanfront construction in La Jolla?**
Yes, California requires licensed PE design for coastal foundations, and the Coastal Commission requires professional engineering analysis for CDP applications.

**How much does oceanfront construction cost in La Jolla?**
Total construction costs for oceanfront homes range from $800-$1,500+ per square foot. Foundation engineering costs $20,000-$60,000 depending on complexity.

**What is a Coastal Development Permit (CDP)?**
CDPs are required for most development in the Coastal Zone. The California Coastal Commission or City of San Diego (with certified LCP) reviews applications for coastal resource protection.

**How long does Coastal Commission approval take?**
CDP timelines vary from 3-12 months or longer depending on project complexity and whether it requires Commission hearing.

**How do you protect against corrosion?**
We specify marine-grade concrete mixes, epoxy-coated or stainless steel reinforcement, increased concrete cover, and cathodic protection where appropriate.
