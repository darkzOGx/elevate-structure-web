## What Is Bluff-Top Home Engineering?

**Direct Answer:**
Bluff-top home engineering encompasses specialized structural design for homes located near coastal cliff edges, including setback analysis based on erosion rates, foundation systems designed for bluff conditions, structural resistance to coastal wind and seismic forces, and coordination with California Coastal Commission development requirements.

Bluff-top construction in Del Mar requires consideration of:

- **Setback Requirements**: Distance from bluff edge based on erosion projections
- **Foundation Systems**: Designs accommodating variable bluff conditions
- **Coastal Exposure**: Wind, salt spray, and seismic forces
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Coastal Commission permit requirements
- **Long-Term Protection**: Designing for 75-100 year project life
