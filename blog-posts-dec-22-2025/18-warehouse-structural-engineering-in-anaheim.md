Yes, California requires licensed PE design for commercial/industrial buildings. Anaheim Building Division requires PE-stamped plans.

**What is rack anchorage engineering?**
Rack anchorage engineering designs anchor bolts that secure storage racks to concrete floors, preventing collapse during earthquakes.

**How long does warehouse engineering take?**
Small projects require 4-6 weeks, while large warehouses may require 8-12 weeks for complete design.
