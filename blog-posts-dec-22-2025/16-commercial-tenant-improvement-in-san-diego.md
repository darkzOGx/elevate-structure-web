## What Is Tenant Improvement Engineering?

**Direct Answer:**
Tenant improvement engineering encompasses structural design for modifications within leased commercial spaces, including demising walls, mezzanine construction, floor openings, and load-bearing wall modifications. These projects require analysis of existing building systems and design of additions that work within the base building structure while meeting California Building Code requirements.

Common TI structural work includes:

- **Demising Walls**: Walls separating tenant spaces
- **Mezzanines**: Intermediate floors adding usable area
- **Floor Openings**: Stairs or equipment penetrations
- **Heavy Loads**: Special equipment or storage
- **Partition Layout**: Non-load-bearing wall placement
