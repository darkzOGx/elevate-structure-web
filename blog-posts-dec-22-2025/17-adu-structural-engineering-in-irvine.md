Yes, California requires licensed PE design for ADU construction. City of Irvine requires PE-stamped structural plans for permit approval.

**How long does ADU permitting take in Irvine?**
City of Irvine typically processes ADU permits in 30-60 days. Engineering typically requires 2-4 weeks.

**Can I convert my garage to an ADU in Irvine?**
Yes, garage conversions are allowed under California ADU law. No replacement parking is required.

**What is the maximum ADU size allowed in Irvine?**
Detached ADUs can be up to 1,200 square feet. Attached ADUs have limitations based on lot size.
