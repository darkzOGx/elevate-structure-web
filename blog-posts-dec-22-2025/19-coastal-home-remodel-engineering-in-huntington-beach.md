## What Is Coastal Home Remodel Engineering?

**Direct Answer:**
Coastal home remodel engineering encompasses structural modifications for beach and near-beach residences, including load-bearing wall removal for open floor plans, second story additions, and comprehensive renovations using materials resistant to salt air and marine conditions. These projects require analysis of existing structures and design of modifications that improve both function and durability.

Coastal remodel engineering addresses:

- **Open Floor Plans**: Removing walls for ocean views
- **Marine Exposure**: Corrosion-resistant materials
- **Coastal Winds**: Enhanced lateral force design
- **Foundation Conditions**: Sandy soil challenges
- **Code Updates**: Meeting current seismic requirements
