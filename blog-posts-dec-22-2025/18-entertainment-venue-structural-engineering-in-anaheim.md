## What Is Entertainment Venue Structural Engineering?

**Direct Answer:**
Entertainment venue structural engineering encompasses structural design for facilities hosting public entertainment, including large-span roof systems, dynamic crowd loading, stage and rigging support, and specialty ride structures. These projects require sophisticated analysis of unusual loading conditions, vibration control, and life-safety systems supporting high-occupancy venues.
