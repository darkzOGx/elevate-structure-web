## What is Hillside Foundation Engineering in Pacific Palisades?

**Direct Answer:** Hillside foundation engineering in Pacific Palisades encompasses the design of deep foundation systems for homes built on sloped terrain, including caisson (drilled pier) foundations extending to stable soil or bedrock, grade beam systems that span variable soil conditions, and retaining walls creating level building pads on hillside lots.

Pacific Palisades' dramatic canyon topography offers spectacular views but presents significant engineering challenges. Properties in Castellammare, Riviera, and the Highlands often sit on slopes exceeding 30%, requiring foundations that reach 30-60 feet to stable bearing.

Our engineers work with [geotechnical consultants](/services/structural-engineering) to develop foundation solutions specific to each property's unique conditions.
