**Direct Answer:** Commercial structural engineering in San Diego encompasses the design of structural systems for non-residential buildings including offices, retail, restaurants, warehouses, and industrial facilities. This includes steel and concrete frame design, foundation systems for commercial loads, tenant improvement structural modifications, and seismic design meeting California Building Code requirements.
