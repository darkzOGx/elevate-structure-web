## ORANGE COUNTY CITIES

### Irvine
**Population**: 307,670  
**Median Home Value**: $950,000  
**Median Household Income**: $108,000  
**Building Department**: (949) 724-6363  
**Website**: cityofirvine.org/building-safety

**Key Regulations**:
- Title 24 energy compliance required (strict enforcement)
- Seismic Zone 4 - high seismic activity area
- Green building standards for new construction
- Fire-resistant construction requirements for hillside homes
- Strict stormwater management (WQMP required for >10,000 sq ft)

**Construction Market**:
- High-end residential developments
- Major commercial office projects
- Industrial/research facilities (Irvine Spectrum, Irvine Business Complex)
- ADU and renovation projects growing rapidly

**Permit Data**:
- ~3,500 residential permits annually
- Average permit processing: 3-4 weeks
- Plan check fees: $1,500-$4,000 typical residential
- Strong code enforcement

**Engineering Opportunities**:
- Master-planned community developments
- Corporate headquarters structural design
- Seismic retrofit for older buildings (1970s-80s stock)
- Sustainable/LEED certification projects

### Newport Beach
**Population**: 85,239  
**Median Home Value**: $2,100,000  
**Median Household Income**: $127,000  
**Building Department**: (949) 644-3275  
**Website**: newportbeachca.gov/trending/departments/community-development

**Key Regulations**:
- **Coastal Commission approval** required for many projects
- Extremely strict setback requirements (esp. on Balboa Peninsula)
- Flood zone considerations (FEMA Zone VE in many coastal areas)
- Height restrictions (2-3 stories max in most residential zones)
- Coastal bluff engineering requirements
- Marine environment corrosion considerations

**Construction Market**:
- Ultra-luxury residential (custom homes $5M-$50M+)
- Major remodels and additions
- Historic home preservation and seismic upgrades
- Waterfront property engineering challenges

**Permit Data**:
- ~1,200 residential permits annually
- Average permit processing: 6-8 weeks (Coastal Commission adds time)
- Plan check fees: $3,000-$8,000+ for luxury homes
- Among highest permit costs in CA

**Engineering Opportunities**:
- Coastal foundation design (corrosion-resistant, salt spray)
- Seismic retrofit of oceanfront properties
- Luxury custom home structural engineering
- Deck and balcony engineering (stringent wind/seismic loads)
- Deep foundation systems (poor soil conditions near coast)

- "Oceanfront properties in Newport Beach face unique challenges..."
- "With median home values exceeding $2.1M, Newport Beach homeowners expect..."
- "The salt-air environment in Newport Beach requires special consideration..."
