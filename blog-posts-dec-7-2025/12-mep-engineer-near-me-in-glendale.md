## What Is MEP Engineering?

**Direct Answer:** MEP engineering encompasses the design of Mechanical (HVAC), Electrical, and Plumbing systems for buildings. Licensed Professional Engineers analyze building loads, size equipment, design distribution systems, and prepare permit-ready construction documents. MEP systems provide heating, cooling, ventilation, lighting, power, water supply, and drainage essential for building function.

**Mechanical Engineering**
- HVAC system design (heating, ventilation, air conditioning)
- Equipment selection and sizing
- Duct and piping layout
- Energy code compliance (Title 24)
- Ventilation requirements

**Electrical Engineering**
- Power distribution design
- Lighting design and controls
- Panel sizing and circuits
- Emergency power systems
- Low voltage systems

**Plumbing Engineering**
- Domestic water supply
- Sanitary drainage systems
- Gas piping
- Storm drainage
- Specialty systems (medical gas, etc.)
