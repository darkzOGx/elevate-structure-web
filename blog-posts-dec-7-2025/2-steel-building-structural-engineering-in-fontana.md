## Steel Building Types and Structural Engineering Requirements

### Pre-Engineered Metal Buildings (PEMB)

Pre-engineered metal buildings represent a significant portion of Fontana's industrial construction. Our engineering services for PEMB include:

**Foundation Design:** While PEMB manufacturers provide the building structure, foundation engineering requires local expertise. We design foundations appropriate for Fontana's soil conditions, including spread footings, grade beams, and mat foundations as needed.

**Mezzanine and Office Integration:** Adding mezzanines or office areas within PEMB structures requires structural engineering to ensure proper load distribution and connection to the pre-engineered frame.

**Equipment Support:** Fontana's manufacturing and distribution facilities often require overhead cranes, conveyor systems, or heavy equipment. We design structural supports integrated with PEMB buildings.

### Custom Steel Frame Buildings

Custom steel frame construction in Fontana allows for greater architectural flexibility and specific performance requirements:

**Moment Frame Design:** For buildings requiring large openings or specific architectural features, we design steel moment frames that resist lateral forces while accommodating design needs.

**Braced Frame Systems:** Concentrically braced frames (CBF) and eccentrically braced frames (EBF) provide efficient lateral resistance for Fontana's seismic requirements.

**Special Moment Frame Design:** High-seismic applications may require special steel moment frames with specific connection details and material requirements.

### What is the difference between moment frame and braced frame for Fontana buildings?

Moment frames resist lateral forces through the rigid connections between beams and columns, allowing for open floor plans. Braced frames use diagonal members to resist lateral forces, providing efficient but more constrained layouts. Fontana's seismic requirements often make braced frames more economical for industrial buildings.

### Can existing steel buildings in Fontana be expanded or modified?

Yes, existing steel buildings can be expanded or modified with proper structural engineering. We evaluate existing framing, design additions or modifications, and coordinate with the original construction. Many older Fontana industrial buildings undergo tenant improvement or expansion projects.

- Additional bracing
- Foundation strengthening
- Diaphragm improvements
