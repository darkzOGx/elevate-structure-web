## Common Foundation Problems in Brentwood

### Expansive Soil Issues

Brentwood's clay soils expand and contract with moisture:

**Manifestations:**
- Seasonal floor movement
- Sticking doors in wet season
- Diagonal wall cracks
- Foundation heaving

**Contributing Factors:**
- Inadequate drainage
- Large trees near foundation
- Irrigation patterns
- Climate variation

### Settlement Problems

**Differential Settlement:**
- Uneven soil bearing
- Variable moisture conditions
- Organic matter decomposition

**Signs:**
- Sloping floors
- Cracked walls
- Separated corners
- Uneven door frames

### Seismic Vulnerability

Older Brentwood homes often lack:
- Adequate foundation bolting
- Cripple wall bracing
- Proper post connections
- Continuous load paths

## Foundation Repair Solutions

### Pier and Underpinning Systems

**Push Piers:**
- Steel piers driven to bearing stratum
- Lift capability for settlement correction
- Minimal disruption to landscape
- Cost: $1,500-$3,500 per pier

**Helical Piers:**
- Screwed into stable soil
- Immediate load capacity
- Ideal for new construction loads
- Cost: $2,000-$4,000 per pier

### Concrete Foundation Repair

**Crack Injection:**
- Epoxy for structural cracks
- Polyurethane for water infiltration
- Cost: $200-$500 per crack

**Foundation Wall Reinforcement:**
- Carbon fiber strips
- Steel channels
- Cost: $400-$700 per linear foot

### Seismic Retrofitting

**Foundation Bolting:**
- Anchor bolts to sill plate
- Cost: $2,500-$5,000

**Cripple Wall Bracing:**
- Structural plywood sheathing
- Cost: $3,000-$8,000

**Complete Package:**
- Bolting + bracing + connections
- Cost: $7,000-$15,000

### Drainage Improvements

**French Drains:**
- Perimeter drainage collection
- Cost: $3,000-$8,000

**Sump Systems:**
- Pump stations for water management
- Cost: $2,500-$5,000

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much does foundation repair cost in Brentwood?

Foundation repair in Brentwood ranges from $15,000 for minor repairs to $175,000+ for comprehensive renovations. Average projects run $45,000-$100,000. Brentwood's premium market adds 25-35% to typical Los Angeles costs.

### What causes foundation problems in Brentwood?

Brentwood foundations are affected by expansive clay soils, seismic activity near the Santa Monica Fault, inadequate original construction, large tree root systems, and drainage issues. Many older homes lack proper seismic connections.

We recommend seismic evaluation for all pre-1980 Brentwood homes. The Santa Monica Fault proximity creates significant risk. Retrofit typically costs $7,000-$15,000 and provides essential protection for your investment.
