## Foundation Leveling Methods

### Helical Pier Lifting

**Process:**
1. Piers installed to stable bearing
2. Hydraulic jacks attached to pier brackets
3. Controlled lifting to target elevation
4. Brackets locked in lifted position

**Advantages:**
- Permanent support solution
- Precise elevation control
- Minimal site disturbance
- Works on most soil types

**Cost Range:** $1,500-$3,000 per pier

### Push Pier Systems

**Process:**
1. Heavy-duty brackets attached to foundation
2. Steel piers driven to stable soil
3. Building weight used for installation force
4. Hydraulic lifting to level condition

**Advantages:**
- Deep penetration to competent soil
- High load capacity
- Immediate stabilization
- Effective for heavy structures

**Cost Range:** $1,200-$2,500 per pier

### Polyurethane Foam Injection

**Process:**
1. Small holes drilled through slab
2. Expanding foam injected beneath foundation
3. Foam expands and lifts slab
4. Holes patched after completion

**Advantages:**
- Minimally invasive
- Quick completion (often same-day)
- Waterproof material
- Lightweight fill

**Cost Range:** $5-$15 per square foot

### Mudjacking

**Process:**
1. Holes drilled through slab
2. Cement slurry pumped beneath
3. Hydraulic pressure lifts slab
4. Holes patched

**Advantages:**
- Economical option
- Fills voids completely
- Proven long-term performance
- Large area treatment

**Cost Range:** $3-$8 per square foot

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much can a foundation be lifted?

Most residential foundations can be safely lifted 2-4 inches. Greater corrections may require staged lifting over weeks or months to prevent structural damage. Our engineers specify safe lifting limits based on structural analysis and construction type.

### Will leveling fix my cracked walls?

Leveling stabilizes the foundation and prevents additional cracking. Existing cracks may partially close during lifting. Interior and exterior repairs are typically needed after leveling to address cosmetic damage and restore waterproofing.

### How long does foundation leveling take?

Pier installation takes 2-5 days depending on the number required. Foam injection can often be completed in one day. The actual lifting process takes 1-2 days with additional time for any staged lifting requirements.

### Can I stay in my home during leveling?

Yes, most leveling work allows continued occupancy. Pier installation causes minimal vibration. Lifting operations may require temporary furniture movement. Our engineers specify any access restrictions needed during construction.

### Does foundation leveling come with a warranty?

Quality contractors provide warranties on both workmanship and long-term performance. Typical pier warranties run 10-25 years and are often transferable to future owners. We specify warranty requirements in our engineering documents.
