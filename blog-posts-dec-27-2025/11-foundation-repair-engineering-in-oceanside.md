## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is foundation repair engineering in Oceanside?**
Foundation repair engineering includes evaluation, analysis, and PE-stamped design for repairing damaged foundations. This encompasses crack assessment, settlement analysis, underpinning design, and drainage solutions meeting California Building Code requirements.

**How much does foundation repair engineering cost in Oceanside?**
Foundation repair engineering typically costs $2,500-$12,000 depending on damage extent. Simple crack repairs average $2,000-$4,000, comprehensive underpinning $6,000-$15,000+. Engineering costs are typically 5-10% of total repair construction costs.

**What causes foundation problems in Oceanside?**
Common causes include expansive clay soils, poor drainage, inadequate original construction, age-related deterioration, and seismic activity. Coastal properties also face salt air corrosion and higher groundwater challenges.

**Do I need an engineer for foundation cracks?**
Significant cracks wider than 1/4 inch, diagonal cracks, or cracks with vertical displacement should be evaluated by a structural engineer. Hairline cracks may be cosmetic, but professional assessment ensures safety and identifies underlying causes.

**How long does foundation repair take?**
Foundation repair engineering takes 1-3 weeks from assessment to permit-ready plans. Actual repair construction varies from 1-2 weeks for crack repairs to 4-8 weeks for major underpinning projects.
