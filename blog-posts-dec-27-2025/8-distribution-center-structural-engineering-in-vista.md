## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is distribution center structural engineering?**
Distribution center structural engineering includes specialized analysis and PE-stamped documentation for logistics facilities. This encompasses high-capacity floor slab design, building structure, dock facilities, and coordinated rack anchorage meeting California Building Code.

**How much does distribution center engineering cost in Vista?**

**What floor slab design is needed for distribution centers?**
Distribution center floor slabs must support heavy rack loads (250-500 PSF), forklift traffic, and potential automation. Typical designs include 7-8 inch thick slabs with fiber or rebar reinforcing, designed for concentrated rack post loads.
