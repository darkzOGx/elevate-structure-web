## What ADU Foundation Services Are Available in Stanton?

ADU foundation services include detached ADU foundations, garage conversion foundations, and attached ADU tie-ins. The most common services address new detached units and garage conversions.

**Detached ADU Foundation Design**
New detached ADUs require independent foundations designed for building loads and local soil conditions. Our designs include slab reinforcing, anchor bolts, and hold-down connections.

**Garage Conversion Foundation**
Converting garages to ADUs often requires foundation modifications for new wall locations, bathroom additions, and code compliance. Our designs address existing slab conditions.

**Attached ADU Foundation**
ADUs attached to existing homes require foundation tie-ins preventing differential settlement. Our designs ensure proper connections between old and new construction.

**Foundation Assessment for Conversions**
Existing garage slabs may need evaluation and reinforcement. Our assessments verify adequacy for ADU use.
