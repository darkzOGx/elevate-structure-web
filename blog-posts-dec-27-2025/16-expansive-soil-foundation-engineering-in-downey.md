## What Is Expansive Soil Foundation Engineering in Downey?

Expansive soil foundation engineering encompasses specialized design for foundations in soils that change volume with moisture content. In Downey, this includes deeper footings, reinforced slabs, moisture barriers, and drainage systems meeting California Building Code requirements for expansive soil conditions.

**How do you identify expansive soils?**
Expansive soils are identified through geotechnical testing measuring plasticity index and expansion potential. Visual indicators include surface cracking in dry periods and sticky clay when wet. Most Downey properties have some expansive soil conditions.
