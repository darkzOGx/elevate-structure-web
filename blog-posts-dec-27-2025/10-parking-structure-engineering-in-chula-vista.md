## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is parking structure engineering?**
Parking structure engineering includes specialized analysis and PE-stamped documentation for multi-level parking facilities. This encompasses concrete or steel frame design, ramp systems, durability detailing, and seismic design meeting ACI 362 and CBC requirements.

**How much does parking structure engineering cost in Chula Vista?**
Parking structure engineering costs $25,000-$80,000+ depending on size. Small structures average $20,000-$35,000, large multi-level garages $55,000-$100,000+.

**What makes parking structure design different from other buildings?**
Parking structures face unique challenges including chloride exposure from vehicles, thermal movement from open exposure, heavy concentrated wheel loads, and impact from vehicle collisions. Specialized durability detailing is essential for long service life.
