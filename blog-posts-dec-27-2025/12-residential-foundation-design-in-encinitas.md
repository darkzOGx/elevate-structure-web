## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is residential foundation design in Encinitas?**

**How much does residential foundation design cost in Encinitas?**
Residential foundation design costs $2,500-$8,000 depending on complexity. Standard foundations average $2,000-$3,500, hillside or coastal properties $5,000-$12,000+.

**What foundation types are used in Encinitas?**
Common foundation types include slab-on-grade for flat lots, raised wood floors for drainage, and deep foundations (caissons or piles) for hillside and coastal bluff properties. The geotechnical report typically recommends appropriate foundation systems.

**Do I need a geotechnical report for foundation design?**
Yes, Encinitas typically requires geotechnical reports for new construction establishing soil conditions and foundation recommendations. Foundation engineers design based on these recommendations.
