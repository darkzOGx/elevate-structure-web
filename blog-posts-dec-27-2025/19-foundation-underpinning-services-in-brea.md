## What Underpinning Services Are Available in Brea?

Underpinning services include helical pier installation design, push pier systems, traditional concrete underpinning, and foundation lifting. The most common services address residential foundation settlement and hillside foundation stabilization.

**Helical Pier Underpinning**
Helical piers are screwed into competent bearing soils with minimal excavation. Our designs specify pier sizes, depths, and bracket connections.

**Push Pier Systems**
Hydraulically driven push piers provide high-capacity support driven to bedrock or competent soils. Our designs coordinate with manufacturer specifications.

**Concrete Underpinning**
Traditional mass concrete underpinning extends foundations by sequential excavation and concrete placement. Our designs ensure structural continuity.

**Foundation Lifting**
Some underpinning systems allow lifting settled structures back toward original positions. Our designs address lifting sequences and structural implications.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is foundation underpinning?**
Foundation underpinning involves extending or adding support to existing foundations experiencing settlement. This includes helical piers, push piers, and concrete underpinning extending foundations to competent bearing materials.

**How much does underpinning engineering cost in Brea?**
Underpinning engineering costs $4,000-$15,000 depending on extent. Partial underpinning averages $4,000-$8,000, comprehensive underpinning $8,000-$18,000+.

**When is underpinning needed?**
Underpinning is needed when foundations are settling due to inadequate bearing, soil shrinkage, or construction on fill. Signs include progressive cracking, sticking doors, and sloping floors.
