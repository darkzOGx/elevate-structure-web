**What causes foundation settlement in Poway?**
Common causes include expansive clay soils, poor drainage, inadequate original construction, age-related deterioration, and drought-related soil shrinkage. Professional analysis identifies specific causes for each property.

**When should I get a foundation assessment?**
Seek assessment when you notice cracks wider than 1/4 inch, sticking doors/windows, sloping floors, or gaps between walls and ceilings. Pre-purchase evaluations are recommended for older homes.
