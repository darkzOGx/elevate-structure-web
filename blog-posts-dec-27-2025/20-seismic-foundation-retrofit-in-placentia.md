## What Seismic Foundation Services Are Available in Placentia?

Seismic foundation services include foundation bolting, cripple wall bracing, shear panel installation, and complete house bolting. The most common services address pre-1980s homes lacking adequate seismic connections.

**Foundation Bolt Retrofit**
Adding anchor bolts connects wood sill plates to concrete foundations, preventing homes from sliding off foundations during earthquakes.

**Cripple Wall Bracing**
Short stud walls between foundations and first floors are vulnerable to collapse. Plywood sheathing and blocking strengthen these walls.

**Shear Panel Installation**
Plywood shear panels transfer seismic forces from structure to foundation, completing the load path.

**Complete Retrofit Packages**
