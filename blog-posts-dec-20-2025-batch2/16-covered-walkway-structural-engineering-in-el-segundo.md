## What Is Covered Walkway Structural Engineering?

**Direct Answer:**
Covered walkway structural engineering involves designing post, beam, and roof systems for linear structures connecting buildings or areas of a property. In El Segundo, walkway designs address seismic and wind loads, connection to adjacent structures, and foundation requirements while meeting building codes.

Covered walkways range from simple open-sided pergola structures to fully enclosed corridors with walls and roofing.
