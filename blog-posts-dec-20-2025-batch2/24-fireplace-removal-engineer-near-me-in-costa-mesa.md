## Why Do You Need a Fireplace Removal Engineer?

**Direct Answer:**
Fireplace removal engineers evaluate structural implications of removing chimneys that may support roof loads, design floor and ceiling repairs after masonry removal, ensure remaining structure has adequate support, and address any load path modifications. ## What Fireplace Removal Services Are Available?

**Direct Answer:**
Fireplace removal engineering services include structural assessment of chimney removal impacts, floor framing repair design, ceiling/roof support modifications, full chimney versus partial removal analysis, and permit documentation for Costa Mesa building department.

**Full Chimney Removal**
Removing chimney from foundation through roof. Requires evaluation of roof support and floor/ceiling repair.

**Partial Removal**
Removing fireplace and lower chimney while leaving upper portion. May be simpler but requires proper support for remaining structure.

**Floor Space Reclamation**
Design of floor framing to fill the void left by fireplace removal, creating usable floor space.
