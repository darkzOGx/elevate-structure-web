## What Are Common Renovation Modifications?

**Direct Answer:**
Common modifications include load-bearing wall removals for open floor plans, header enlargements for bigger windows, floor system strengthening for new tile or stone, roof structure modifications for vaulted ceilings, foundation repairs and seismic upgrades, and second-story additions.

**Wall Removals**
Opening up floor plans by removing interior walls. Requires beam design and often foundation modifications for new posts.

**Window and Door Enlargements**
Larger openings require header engineering. Coastal views make window expansions popular in Hermosa Beach.

**Floor Strengthening**
Upgrading from carpet to heavy tile requires floor system evaluation and potential reinforcement.

**Seismic Upgrades**
Older Hermosa Beach homes often need foundation bolting, cripple wall bracing, and shear wall improvements during renovations.
