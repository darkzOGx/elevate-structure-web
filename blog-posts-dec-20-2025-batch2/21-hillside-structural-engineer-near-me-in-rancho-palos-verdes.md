## What Services Do Hillside Structural Engineers Provide?

**Direct Answer:**
Hillside structural engineers provide retaining wall design, foundation systems for sloped sites, slope stabilization engineering, new hillside home structural design, hillside addition engineering, and drainage systems that protect slopes and structures.

**Retaining Walls**
Design of walls that hold back hillside soil, allowing level building areas and preventing slope failures. Options include gravity walls, cantilever walls, and mechanically stabilized earth systems.

**Hillside Foundations**
Deep foundation systems, grade beams, and caisson designs that anchor homes securely to stable soil or rock below unstable surface materials.

**Slope Stabilization**
Engineering solutions for active or potential slope movement including dewatering, soil nailing, and tie-back systems.

Hillside projects involve slope stability analysis, specialized foundation systems, retaining structures, and drainage that standard flat-lot construction doesn't require.

Yes, Rancho Palos Verdes requires geotechnical investigation for most hillside projects to evaluate soil conditions and slope stability.

Hillside permits in Rancho Palos Verdes typically require 8-16 weeks due to additional reviews for slope stability and geological hazards.
