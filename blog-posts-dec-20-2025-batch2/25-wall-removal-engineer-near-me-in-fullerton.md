## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I know if a wall is load-bearing?**
Professional engineering determines wall function. Signs include walls running perpendicular to ceiling joists, walls below other walls, and walls with plumbing or HVAC.

**Can any wall be removed?**
Most walls can be removed with proper engineering. Some require extensive beams and foundation modifications.

**How long does wall removal engineering take?**
