## What Does MEP Engineering Include in Bonita Projects?

**Direct Answer:**
MEP engineering includes three core disciplines: Mechanical (HVAC, ventilation, temperature control), Electrical (power distribution, lighting, low voltage systems), and Plumbing (water supply, drainage, gas piping). Each discipline requires specific calculations, equipment selection, and code-compliant design documentation for permit approval.

**Mechanical Engineering (HVAC)**
Heating, ventilation, and air conditioning design for Bonita's climate. This includes load calculations, equipment sizing, duct design, and energy modeling to meet Title 24 requirements. Our mechanical engineers design systems optimized for San Diego County's temperate climate, which often allows for energy-efficient solutions.

**Electrical Engineering**
Power distribution, lighting design, and low-voltage systems including fire alarm, security, and communications infrastructure. Bonita electrical designs must comply with the California Electrical Code and coordinate with San Diego Gas & Electric requirements.

**Plumbing Engineering**
Water supply, sanitary drainage, storm drainage, and natural gas systems. Our plumbing designs account for local water pressure conditions, sewer connection requirements, and California's water efficiency standards.
