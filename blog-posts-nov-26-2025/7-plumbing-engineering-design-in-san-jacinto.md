## What Is Plumbing Engineering Design in California?

**Direct Answer:**
Plumbing engineering design involves the planning and documentation of water supply, sanitary drainage, storm drainage, and gas piping systems for buildings. ## What Does Plumbing Engineering Include in San Jacinto Projects?

**Direct Answer:**
Plumbing engineering includes water supply design (sizing, pressure calculations, water heater selection), sanitary drainage design (fixture units, pipe sizing, venting), storm drainage connections, gas piping layout, and fire suppression coordination when required. Each system must be engineered for adequate capacity while meeting California's water efficiency requirements.

**Water Supply Design**
Sizing of water service, distribution piping, and fixture connections based on fixture unit calculations and required pressures. Design accounts for San Jacinto's water pressure conditions and EMWD service requirements.

**Sanitary Drainage Design**
Gravity drainage system layout including pipe sizing, slopes, cleanout locations, and vent configurations. Systems must handle peak flows while meeting California Plumbing Code requirements.

**Water Efficiency Compliance**
Selection of fixtures meeting CALGreen water efficiency requirements and documentation for Title 24 compliance. California's water conservation mandates affect fixture selection throughout San Jacinto projects.

**Gas Piping Design**
Natural gas distribution for heating, cooking, and water heating equipment. Sizing calculations ensure adequate pressure at all appliances while meeting SoCalGas requirements.
