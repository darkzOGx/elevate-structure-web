## What Does a Garage Conversion Engineer Do in Huntington Beach?

**Direct Answer:** A garage conversion engineer evaluates existing garage structures, designs structural modifications required for habitable occupancy, and provides PE-stamped plans meeting California Building Code requirements. ## What Structural Work Is Required for Garage Conversions?

**Direct Answer:** Typical garage conversion structural work includes foundation modifications or verification, wall framing changes to replace garage doors with standard walls, roof structure reinforcement for insulation loads, and connection details ensuring seismic performance. Scope varies based on existing construction and proposed use.

**Foundation Modifications**
Existing garage slabs were designed for vehicle loads but may not meet habitable space requirements for:
- Thermal insulation
- Moisture protection
- Perimeter foundation depth

Many conversions require new perimeter foundations to support converted walls. Our engineers evaluate existing conditions and design appropriate solutions.

**Wall Framing**
Converting garage door openings to solid walls requires structural framing including:
- Header removal or modification
- New wall framing installation
- Shear wall additions for seismic resistance
- Connection details to existing structure

**Roof Modifications**
Adding insulation to garage roofs may require structural reinforcement if existing framing cannot support additional loads. Our engineers verify capacity and design reinforcement as needed.
