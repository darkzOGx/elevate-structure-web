## What Are Yorba Linda's Unique Commercial Engineering Considerations?

Yorba Linda sits at the transition between the Los Angeles Basin and the Chino Hills, creating varied soil conditions across the city. Eastern Yorba Linda near Carbon Canyon and the Yorba Linda Country Club area features hillside terrain with bedrock at relatively shallow depths.
