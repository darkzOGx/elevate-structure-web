## What Structural Engineering Services Are Required for Bedroom Additions?

**Direct Answer:**
Bedroom addition projects require foundation design, floor framing analysis, wall and roof structural design, load path engineering, and connection details. Additional services include seismic load analysis per California Building Code, gravity load calculations, and coordination with existing structural systems. Projects near the coast may require corrosion-resistant material specifications.

**Foundation Engineering**
Every bedroom addition needs proper foundation support. In Seal Beach, foundation options include extending existing foundations, new concrete stem walls, or post-and-beam systems depending on site conditions and addition size.

**Framing Design**
Structural engineers design floor joists, wall studs, headers, and roof framing to support code-required loads. Seal Beach additions must meet Title 24 energy requirements, which often affect wall thickness and framing specifications.

**Connection Details**
Critical connection points between new and existing structures require engineered solutions. This includes ledger connections, hold-downs, and anchor bolt specifications that ensure the addition behaves as an integrated structure during earthquakes.
