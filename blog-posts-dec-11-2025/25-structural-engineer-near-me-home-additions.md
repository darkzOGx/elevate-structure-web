## Frequently Asked Questions

### When do I need a structural engineer for my addition?
Most additions require engineering for permits. Any project involving foundation work, load-bearing modifications, or second stories definitely needs PE-stamped plans.

### How long does addition engineering take?
Simple additions take 2-3 weeks. Second stories require 4-6 weeks. Complex projects may need longer. We offer expedited service for urgent timelines.

### Do I need both an architect and engineer?
Many additions benefit from both. Some straightforward projects can proceed with engineering only. We can advise on what your project needs.

### Will my foundation support a second story?
Foundation evaluation determines capacity. Many foundations require some reinforcement for second stories—this is common and usually manageable.
