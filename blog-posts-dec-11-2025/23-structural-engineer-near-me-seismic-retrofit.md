## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is seismic retrofit required for my building?
Requirements depend on your city and building type. Many cities mandate soft story retrofit for multi-family buildings. Single-family retrofit is typically voluntary but recommended.

### How do I know if my home needs retrofit?
Homes on raised foundations with unbraced cripple walls and unbolted sills are most vulnerable. Professional assessment identifies specific vulnerabilities.

### How long does retrofit take?
Single-family retrofit typically requires 2-4 weeks construction. Soft story retrofit takes 4-8 weeks. Engineering takes 4-8 weeks before construction.
