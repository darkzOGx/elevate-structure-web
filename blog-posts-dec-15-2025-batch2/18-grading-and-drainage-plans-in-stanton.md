## What Is Grading and Drainage Engineering?

Grading and drainage engineering involves designing the land surface and drainage systems to direct water away from structures and manage stormwater runoff. Licensed Professional Engineers design grading plans, drainage systems, and erosion control measures that meet local requirements and protect properties from water damage.

Proper grading and drainage is fundamental to protecting buildings, preventing erosion, and managing stormwater in compliance with Stanton and Orange County regulations.

## What Does Grading and Drainage Engineering Include?

**Site Grading Design**
- Finish grade elevations
- Slope design and analysis
- Pad elevations and building placement
- Earthwork quantities

**Surface Drainage Systems**
- Swales and berms
- Area drains
- Gutter and downspout collection
- Positive drainage around structures

**Subsurface Drainage**
- French drains
- Perimeter drains
- Subgrade drainage
- High water table management

**Stormwater Management**
- Retention/detention design
- Low impact development (LID) features
- Bioswales and planters
- Stormwater quality treatment
