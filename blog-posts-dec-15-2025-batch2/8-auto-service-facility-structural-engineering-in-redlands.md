## What Is Auto Service Facility Structural Engineering?

Auto service facility structural engineering involves designing buildings and structural elements that accommodate vehicle service operations, including lift equipment, vehicle loads, overhead clearances, and the specialized floor and drainage systems these facilities require.

Key engineering considerations include:
- In-ground and above-ground lift anchorage
- Floor slab design for vehicle and equipment loads
- Clear span requirements for service bays
- Overhead door and crane beam support
- Drainage and containment systems
