## Why Do South Orange County Properties Need Coastal Foundation Engineers?

Coastal environments present unique foundation challenges that require specialized engineering:

- **Corrosive salt air and soil** attacking standard steel reinforcement
- **Sandy, loose soils** with poor bearing capacity
- **High water tables** affecting foundation construction
- **Flood zone requirements** in certain areas
- **Coastal Commission jurisdiction** for beachfront properties

Professional coastal foundation engineering addresses these challenges with appropriate materials, foundation types, and protective measures.
