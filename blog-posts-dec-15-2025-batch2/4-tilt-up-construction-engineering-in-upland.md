## What Is Tilt-Up Construction Engineering?

Tilt-up construction engineering involves the design of concrete wall panel systems that are cast on-site and tilted into position to form building walls. Licensed Professional Engineers design panel thickness, reinforcement, connections, and coordinate with roof framing systems to create efficient, economical structures for industrial and commercial applications.

This construction method is particularly popular in the Inland Empire due to its cost-effectiveness for large floor plate buildings like warehouses and distribution centers. California Building Code requirements and seismic design provisions require careful engineering attention to panel-to-roof connections, which have historically been vulnerable in earthquakes.
