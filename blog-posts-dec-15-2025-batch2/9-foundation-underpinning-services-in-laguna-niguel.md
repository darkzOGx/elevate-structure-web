## What Is Foundation Underpinning?

Foundation underpinning involves extending or strengthening existing foundations to increase load-carrying capacity, address settlement issues, or stabilize foundations affected by soil movement. Licensed Professional Engineers design underpinning systems that transfer building loads to more competent soil or rock strata below the original foundation level.

This specialized engineering is particularly relevant in Laguna Niguel's hillside communities where expansive soils, slope movement, and settlement can affect foundation performance over time.

## When Is Foundation Underpinning Needed in South Orange County?

**Settlement Problems**
Differential settlement causing cracks in walls, doors/windows that won't close, or sloping floors indicates potential need for underpinning.

**Building Additions**
Adding second stories or significant additions may exceed the capacity of original foundations, requiring underpinning to support increased loads.

**Excavation Adjacent to Foundations**
New construction or excavation near existing buildings can undermine foundations, requiring underpinning for protection.

**Hillside Slope Movement**
Soil creep or slope movement affecting hillside properties often requires foundation stabilization through underpinning.

**Change of Building Use**
Converting buildings to higher-load uses may require foundation strengthening.

## What Underpinning Methods Are Used in Laguna Niguel?

**Mass Concrete Underpinning**
Traditional method involving sequential excavation beneath existing foundations and placement of new concrete. Suitable for accessible foundations with stable soil conditions.

**Micropile Underpinning**
Small-diameter drilled piles installed through or adjacent to existing foundations, transferring loads to deep bearing strata. Effective in Laguna Niguel's variable soil conditions.

**Helical Pier Underpinning**
Steel screw piles installed with minimal vibration and excavation, ideal for residential underpinning where access is limited.

**Push Pier Systems**
Steel piers driven through existing foundations to load-bearing strata. Quick installation with immediate load transfer capability.
