## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can all Beverly Hills homes have basement conversions?
Most homes with existing basements can be converted. Homes without basements can sometimes add them, but this is a major excavation project.

### How long does basement conversion take?
Engineering takes 4-8 weeks. Construction varies widely from 3 months for simple conversions to 12+ months for complex projects.

### Is floor lowering possible in my basement?
Usually yes, through underpinning the existing foundation. This is a significant undertaking requiring careful engineering and specialized contractors.

### What about moisture and waterproofing?
Waterproofing is critical for any basement conversion. We design comprehensive systems including drainage, membranes, and sump systems.

### Do I need geological studies?
For significant excavation or in certain Beverly Hills areas, geological studies may be required. We advise on requirements for your specific project.
