## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can any house have a second story added?
Most houses can have a second story added with proper engineering, but costs vary significantly based on existing foundation and framing conditions.

### How long does a second story addition take?
Engineering takes 6-10 weeks. Construction typically takes 6-12 months depending on scope.

### Will I need to move out during construction?
Usually yes, at least during certain phases. The extent of required reinforcement determines how much disruption to expect.

### How much weight does a second story add?
A second story typically adds 40-60 pounds per square foot, effectively doubling the load on existing foundations.

### What if my foundation can't support a second story?
Foundations can be reinforced through underpinning, sister footings, or other methods. We evaluate options and costs.
