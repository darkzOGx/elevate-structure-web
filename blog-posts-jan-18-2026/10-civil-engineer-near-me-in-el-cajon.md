**Problem:** Properties on slopes require significant grading to create usable building areas. Cut and fill operations must balance earthwork, create stable slopes, and manage drainage.

**El Cajon Example:** A residential property on a moderate slope required grading to create a building pad while minimizing retaining walls and maintaining positive drainage.

**Solution:** Integrated grading design:

- Design appropriate slope gradients

**Challenge 2: Drainage Management**

**Problem:** Development changes natural drainage patterns. Increased impervious surfaces create more runoff requiring management to prevent downstream impacts.

**Solution:** Comprehensive drainage design:
- Pre- and post-development analysis

- Design adequate separation
- Plan for future maintenance access

**Problem:** Site development must comply with local grading ordinances, stormwater regulations, and environmental requirements. Requirements vary by jurisdiction and project type.
