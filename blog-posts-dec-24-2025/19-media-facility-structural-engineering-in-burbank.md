## What Are Common Media Facility Challenges in Burbank?

Media facility development in Burbank presents unique challenges requiring experienced engineering solutions.

**Challenge 1: Heavy Rigging Grid Loads**

**Problem:** Modern production requires grid systems supporting concentrated loads of 100+ lbs/SF, creating significant structural demands for support systems.

**Burbank Example:** A new virtual production stage required grid capacity for LED volume panels weighing 30,000+ lbs suspended from multiple points.

**Solution:** Purpose-designed grid support systems with appropriate steel framing, connections, and load distribution for heavy rigging requirements.

**Challenge 2: Acoustic Isolation**

**Problem:** Sound stages require isolation from external noise and between adjacent stages, requiring specialized structural approaches.

**Solution:** Isolated foundation systems, decoupled wall assemblies, and careful structural detailing to prevent sound transmission paths.

**Challenge 3: Column-Free Spans**

**Problem:** Production requires large column-free areas, with major stages requiring 100+ foot clear spans.

**Solution:** Long-span structural systems using trusses, space frames, or arched systems designed for both gravity and seismic loads.
