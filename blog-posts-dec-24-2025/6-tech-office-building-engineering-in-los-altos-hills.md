## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is tech office building engineering?**
Tech office building engineering in Los Altos Hills focuses on accessory structures and residential additions designed for professional work, including detached office buildings, home offices, and technology-equipped workspaces. Structural engineers design these to meet building codes while satisfying design review requirements.

**How much does tech office engineering cost in Los Altos Hills?**
Tech office structural engineering in Los Altos Hills typically costs $12,000-$65,000 depending on project size and complexity. Small accessory structures run $12,000-$25,000. Complete construction costs range $200,000-$1,000,000+ depending on size and finish level.

**Can I build a commercial office in Los Altos Hills?**
Los Altos Hills is residentially zoned without commercial districts. However, accessory structures supporting home-based work are permitted within regulations governing size, placement, and use intensity. These require design review approval.

**How long does design review take in Los Altos Hills?**
Los Altos Hills design review typically requires 8-14 weeks including staff review and Design Review Commission meeting. Complex projects or those requiring environmental review may take longer.

**What about technology infrastructure for home offices?**
Structural engineers coordinate with MEP engineers and IT consultants to accommodate power requirements, cooling systems, and communication infrastructure. Structures are designed for equipment loads and conduit pathways.

**Do I need a structural engineer for a home office addition?**
Yes, California requires PE-stamped structural plans for additions to existing buildings. Building departments require licensed Professional Engineer approval of structural modifications and new construction.
