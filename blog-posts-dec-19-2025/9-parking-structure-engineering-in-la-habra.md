## What Is Parking Structure Engineering?

**Direct Answer:** Parking structure engineering involves designing multi-level parking facilities including foundations, columns, floor slabs, ramps, and lateral systems. California-licensed Professional Engineers design structures using post-tensioned concrete, precast concrete, or steel framing systems optimized for vehicular loading and durability requirements.

Parking structures require specialized design for vehicle loads, ramp geometry, and durability in exposed conditions. Common systems include post-tensioned concrete for long spans, precast double-tees for speed of construction, and steel framing for special conditions.
