## What Are Unique Cold Storage Structural Challenges?

**Direct Answer:** Unique challenges include preventing frost heave under refrigerated slabs, managing thermal movement at panel connections, designing floor slabs for freeze-thaw durability, and accommodating heavy racking loads in demanding environments.

**Frost Heave Prevention**

Heated sub-slab systems or ventilated floor construction prevents ground freezing beneath refrigerated slabs, which can cause significant structural damage.

**Thermal Movement**

Large temperature differentials create thermal movement that must be accommodated at connections between cold and ambient zones.

**Floor Slab Durability**

Refrigerated floor slabs require special concrete mixes, reinforcement, and joint details for durability under freeze-thaw cycling.
