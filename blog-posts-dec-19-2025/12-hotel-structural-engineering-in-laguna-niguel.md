## What Is Hotel Structural Engineering?

**Direct Answer:** Hotel structural engineering involves designing structural systems for hospitality buildings including foundations, multi-story framing, amenity structures, and parking facilities. California-licensed Professional Engineers design efficient repetitive floor systems while accommodating lobbies, restaurants, pools, and other specialized hotel spaces.

Hotels require structural systems that efficiently repeat guest room layouts across multiple floors while accommodating public spaces with different structural requirements. Common construction types include wood-frame over podium, steel frame, and concrete construction.
