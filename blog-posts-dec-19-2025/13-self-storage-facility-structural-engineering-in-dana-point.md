## What Is Self-Storage Facility Structural Engineering?

**Direct Answer:** Self-storage facility structural engineering involves designing structural systems for storage buildings including foundations, framing, corridor systems, and exterior enclosures. California-licensed Professional Engineers design efficient repetitive structures optimized for the light loading typical of storage facilities.

Self-storage buildings require cost-effective structural systems due to competitive market economics. Common construction types include pre-engineered metal buildings for single-story and light-gauge steel or concrete for multi-story facilities.
