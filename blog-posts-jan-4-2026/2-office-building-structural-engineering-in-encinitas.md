Yes, California requires licensed Professional Engineers to design and stamp commercial building structures. The City of Encinitas Building Department requires PE-stamped structural plans for all new office construction and significant tenant improvements.

### What foundation types are common for Encinitas office buildings?
Foundation selection depends on site conditions. Spread footings work for competent soils, while drilled piers or mat foundations address expansive soils or variable conditions. Geotechnical reports guide foundation design decisions.
