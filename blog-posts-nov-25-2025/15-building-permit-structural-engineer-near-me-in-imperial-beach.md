**How long does it take to get a building permit in Imperial Beach?**

**What's included in PE-stamped structural plans?**
PE-stamped plans include structural drawings showing framing, foundations, and connections, plus the engineer's stamp certifying code compliance.

**Do I need separate permits for different work?**
You may need building, electrical, plumbing, and mechanical permits depending on your project scope. Your contractor or engineer can advise on requirements.

**Can I start construction before permits are approved?**
No. Starting construction without permits violates building codes and can result in stop-work orders, fines, and required removal of unpermitted work.
