### Does my Riverside home need seismic retrofit?

Homes built before 1980 on raised foundations are prime candidates for seismic retrofit. Signs you may need retrofit include: no visible foundation bolts, short cripple walls without bracing, or living near active faults like the San Jacinto. We provide free assessments to determine your needs.

### How long does seismic retrofit engineering take?

Seismic retrofit engineering typically takes 2-3 weeks for standard projects. Complex retrofits or homes requiring additional assessment may take 3-4 weeks. This is engineering time only; construction and permits are additional.

### What is foundation bolting?

Foundation bolting secures your home's wood frame to its concrete foundation using anchor bolts and metal connectors. Without proper bolting, your house can slide off its foundation during an earthquake. Retrofitting adds modern bolting to homes built before this was required.
