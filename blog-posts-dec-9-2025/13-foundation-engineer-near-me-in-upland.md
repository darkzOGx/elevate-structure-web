### What causes foundation problems in Upland?

Common causes include expansive clay soils that shrink and swell with moisture changes, poor drainage directing water toward foundations, tree roots, inadequate original construction, and seismic activity. Our assessment identifies causes and solutions.

### Can foundation cracks be repaired without full replacement?

Many foundation cracks can be repaired without replacement. Our engineers evaluate crack type, cause, and severity to determine appropriate repairs. Options include epoxy injection, carbon fiber reinforcement, underpinning, or targeted repair rather than full replacement.
