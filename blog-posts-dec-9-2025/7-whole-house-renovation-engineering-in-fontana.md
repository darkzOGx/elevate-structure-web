### Can I do a whole house renovation in phases?

### Will my renovation trigger code upgrades?

Major renovations often trigger requirements to upgrade portions of the home to current codes. The extent depends on renovation scope and local interpretation. Common triggers include seismic bracing, electrical panel upgrades, and energy code compliance. We identify these requirements early in the design process.

### How do I know if walls are load bearing in my Fontana home?

Determining load-bearing status requires analysis of the home's structural system‚Äînot just wall location. Walls running parallel to floor joists and roof trusses are often non-bearing, while walls supporting beams, perpendicular joists, or second floors are typically bearing. Our engineering assessment identifies all bearing conditions.
