### Can my older home support a second story addition?
Many older Huntington Beach homes can support second story additions with appropriate structural upgrades. ### What happens if my existing foundation can't support an addition?
If existing foundations cannot support additional loads, options include foundation upgrade (underpinning), designing the addition with its own independent foundation, or modifying the addition scope. Your structural engineer evaluates options and recommends cost-effective solutions.

### Do I need architectural plans before structural engineering?
Having architectural plans before structural engineering is typical but not always required. Some engineers can work from sketches or preliminary concepts. However, final structural engineering requires coordinated architectural plans. Design team coordination produces better results than sequential work.

### How do addition requirements differ for coastal Huntington Beach properties?
Coastal Huntington Beach properties may have additional considerations including corrosion protection for structural elements, potential flood zone requirements, and Coastal Commission jurisdiction in some areas. Your engineer evaluates which additional requirements apply to your specific location.
