## What Civil Engineering Requirements Apply to Brentwood Projects?

**Direct Answer:** Brentwood civil engineering projects must comply with Los Angeles Municipal Code grading provisions, Hillside Construction Regulations, storm water management requirements, and specific requirements for Brentwood-Pacific Palisades area. The hillside location triggers additional requirements not applicable to flat-lot development.

**Hillside Grading Regulations**
Los Angeles Hillside Construction Regulations limit grading quantities, establish setbacks from slopes, and impose specific drainage requirements. Grading permits require detailed plans showing existing conditions, proposed grading, and erosion control. Significant grading triggers additional review.

**Retaining Wall Requirements**
Retaining walls exceeding certain heights require permits with structural and civil engineering. Walls must address soil pressures, drainage behind walls, and overall site stability. Multiple terraced walls may have different requirements than single tall walls.

**Storm Water Management**
Los Angeles requires storm water management for development projects. Low Impact Development (LID) requirements may apply depending on project scope. Storm water must be managed on-site or conveyed to approved disposal points without affecting neighboring properties.

**Haul Route Requirements**
Significant grading projects requiring soil import or export must establish approved haul routes. Haul route permits address truck traffic, hours of operation, and route restrictions. Planning for haul routes early prevents project delays.
