**How much does room addition structural engineering cost near me in Irvine?**

**Do I need a structural engineer for a room addition?**
Yes, all room additions in California require structural engineering. Building permits require PE-stamped structural plans showing foundation design, framing, connections to existing structure, and lateral force resistance.

**How long does room addition engineering take in Irvine?**
Room addition structural engineering typically takes 2-4 weeks from initial consultation to permit-ready plans. City of Irvine plan check adds 2-3 weeks. Total timeline from start to permit is typically 4-7 weeks.

**Can my existing foundation support an addition?**
Most existing foundations can support properly designed additions. Our engineers evaluate your existing foundation and design appropriate connections or extensions. In some cases, existing foundation reinforcement may be needed.

**What's involved in connecting new and old roofs?**
Roof integration requires careful design of the junction between new and existing framing. This includes valley framing, ridge connections, rafter ties, and weatherproofing details. Our engineers design connections that ensure structural integrity and weather resistance.

**Will my addition need to match my existing construction?**
Additions should be compatible with existing construction but don't need to be identical. Our engineers design foundations and framing that integrate with your existing home while meeting current code requirements.
