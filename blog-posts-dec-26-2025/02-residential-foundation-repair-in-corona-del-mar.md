## What Are Common Foundation Problems in Corona del Mar?

**Direct Answer:**
Common foundation problems include settlement-related cracking, marine corrosion of reinforcement, drainage-related moisture damage, and seismic-induced movement. Corona del Mar's coastal location exposes foundations to salt air corrosion and sandy soils that may compact or shift, while proximity to fault systems creates ongoing seismic stress.

**Settlement and Cracking**
Sandy coastal soils may compact over time, causing differential settlement that manifests as cracks in foundations, walls, and around windows and doors. Properties in Cameo Highlands and along Ocean Boulevard frequently experience settlement issues.

**Marine Corrosion**
Salt air and moisture penetrate concrete, corroding reinforcing steel. This corrosion causes concrete spalling and structural weakening. Oceanfront properties face accelerated deterioration without proper protection.

**Drainage Problems**
Improper drainage allows water accumulation around foundations, saturating soils and causing erosion, settlement, and moisture intrusion. Many Corona del Mar properties benefit from drainage improvements alongside foundation repairs.
