## What Is Industrial Steel Frame Engineering?

**Direct Answer:**
Industrial steel frame engineering encompasses professional design of structural steel building systems for industrial applications. In Tustin, licensed Professional Engineers design steel moment frames, braced frames, and other lateral systems, along with gravity framing, crane systems, and specialized industrial loading requirements while meeting California's stringent seismic codes.
