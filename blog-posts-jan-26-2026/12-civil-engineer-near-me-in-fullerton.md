## Frequently Asked Questions

### What does a civil engineer do for residential projects?

Civil engineers for residential projects design grading plans, drainage systems, driveways, retaining walls, and utility connections. They ensure proper site drainage, erosion control, and compliance with local codes for building permits.

### How much does civil engineering cost in Fullerton?

Civil engineering in Fullerton typically costs $2,500-$8,000 for residential projects and $5,000-$25,000+ for commercial projects. Fees depend on site size, complexity, grading requirements, and permit needs.

### Do I need a civil engineer for a grading permit?

Fullerton requires PE-stamped civil engineering plans for grading permits involving more than 50 cubic yards of cut or fill. Smaller projects may qualify for exemptions. Civil engineers prepare plans that meet city requirements.

### What is included in civil engineering plans?

Civil engineering plans include grading plans, drainage plans, utility plans, erosion control plans, and construction details. These plans show existing and proposed grades, drainage patterns, pipe sizes, and construction specifications.

### How long does civil engineering take in Fullerton?

Civil engineering design typically takes 2-4 weeks for residential projects and 4-8 weeks for commercial projects. Fullerton plan check adds 3-6 weeks. Complex sites with significant grading take longer.
