## Frequently Asked Questions

### What does an MEP engineer do?

MEP engineers design mechanical (HVAC), electrical, and plumbing systems for buildings. They create plans for heating/cooling, electrical distribution, lighting, water supply, and drainage systems that meet building codes and energy requirements.

### How much does MEP engineering cost in Costa Mesa?

MEP engineering in Costa Mesa typically costs $2,500-$8,000 for residential projects and $5,000-$25,000+ for commercial projects. Fees depend on building size, system complexity, and Title 24 compliance requirements.

### Do I need an MEP engineer for my remodel?

MEP engineering is required when adding or modifying HVAC systems, electrical panels, or plumbing in Costa Mesa. Kitchen and bathroom remodels, room additions, and ADUs typically require MEP plans for permit approval.

### What is Title 24 energy compliance?

Title 24 is California's Building Energy Efficiency Standards requiring all new construction and major renovations to meet specific energy performance targets. MEP engineers calculate compliance for HVAC, lighting, and water heating systems.

### How long does MEP design take?

MEP design typically takes 1-3 weeks for residential projects and 3-8 weeks for commercial projects. Costa Mesa plan check adds 2-4 weeks. Coordination with structural and architectural plans affects overall timeline.
