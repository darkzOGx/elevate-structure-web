## What is Industrial Facility Structural Engineering?

Industrial facility structural engineering encompasses the design and analysis of buildings that house manufacturing, production, assembly, and heavy storage operations. These facilities present engineering challenges distinct from typical commercial construction, including concentrated equipment loads, dynamic crane forces, specialized foundation requirements, and operational flexibility demands.

In San Clemente and South Orange County, industrial facility engineering incorporates additional considerations for coastal environment durability, seismic performance in California's active seismic zone, and coordination with sophisticated manufacturing processes. Our structural engineers analyze operational requirements, design efficient structural systems, specify appropriate materials for the environment, and produce PE-stamped construction documents that satisfy Orange County building department requirements.

Industrial facility engineering services include new construction design, facility expansions, crane system integration, mezzanine additions, equipment foundation design, and structural assessments for property acquisition and operational modifications.
