## What is Foundation Engineering?

Foundation engineering is the specialized branch of structural engineering focused on the design, analysis, and remediation of building foundations. This discipline integrates structural analysis with geotechnical understanding to create foundations that safely transfer building loads to the underlying soil while accommodating site-specific conditions.

In Santa Monica, foundation engineering encompasses new foundation design for construction, foundation repair and strengthening, soft-story retrofit foundation components, hillside foundation solutions, and structural assessments for property transactions and insurance claims.

Our structural engineers analyze soil conditions, calculate building loads, design appropriate foundation systems, and produce PE-stamped construction documents that satisfy City of Santa Monica building department requirements.

## Santa Monica Foundation Challenges

### Coastal Soil Conditions

Santa Monica's coastal location creates soil conditions that significantly impact foundation design and performance.

**Coastal Soil Characteristics:**
- Sandy and silty alluvial deposits
- Variable bearing capacity across properties
- Groundwater proximity in lower elevations
- Potential for liquefaction in specific zones
- Expansive clay in some inland areas
- Marine deposits in near-beach locations

Our engineers work closely with geotechnical consultants to address these conditions through appropriate foundation design.

### Soft-Story Building Vulnerabilities

Many Santa Monica apartment buildings and commercial structures were built with open ground floors that create "soft-story" conditions vulnerable to earthquake damage.

**Soft-Story Foundation Issues:**
- Inadequate foundation connections
- Insufficient lateral force transfer
- Weak soil-structure interface
- Non-compliant existing conditions
- Mandatory retrofit requirements

Santa Monica's soft-story retrofit ordinance requires structural upgrades including foundation improvements that our engineers design and document.

### Hillside Foundation Requirements

**Hillside Foundation Considerations:**

Our engineers design hillside foundations that address slope conditions while providing stable building support.

## Foundation Types for Santa Monica Properties

### Spread Footings

Spread footings distribute building loads over soil bearing area and serve many Santa Monica applications.

**Spread Footing Applications:**
- Standard residential construction
- Light commercial buildings
- Sites with adequate bearing capacity
- Moderate groundwater conditions
- Cost-effective solutions

We design spread footing systems sized for specific soil conditions and building loads.

### Continuous Footings

Continuous footings under bearing walls provide efficient support for many building types.

**Continuous Footing Design:**
- Residential foundation walls
- Commercial perimeter conditions
- Basement and retaining walls
- Stepped footings for sloped sites
- Integral grade beam systems

Properly designed continuous footings accommodate both vertical and lateral loads.

### Drilled Pier Foundations

Drilled piers (caissons) reach competent bearing materials below problematic surface soils.

**Drilled Pier Applications:**
- Hillside construction
- Poor near-surface soils
- Heavy concentrated loads
- Liquefaction-prone sites
- Deep bearing requirements

Our engineers design drilled pier systems appropriate for specific site conditions and loading requirements.

### Mat Foundations

Mat (raft) foundations distribute loads across entire building footprints for challenging conditions.

**Mat Foundation Uses:**
- High water table sites
- Variable soil conditions
- Heavy uniform loads
- Settlement control requirements
- Basement construction

Mat foundations provide solutions where conventional footings may not perform adequately.
