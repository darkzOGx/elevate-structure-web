## Common Foundation Problems in Hermosa Beach

### Settlement and Movement

Hermosa Beach foundations frequently experience settlement from sandy coastal soils.

**Settlement Issues:**
- Differential foundation settlement
- Pier and column sinking
- Floor slope development
- Door and window binding
- Crack propagation
- Drainage complications

Our engineers evaluate settlement patterns and design stabilization solutions appropriate for coastal conditions.

### Coastal Corrosion Damage

Salt air exposure accelerates foundation deterioration in Hermosa Beach.

**Corrosion Problems:**
- Reinforcement corrosion
- Concrete spalling
- Anchor bolt degradation
- Connection deterioration
- Seawall integration issues
- Below-grade moisture damage

We design repairs addressing corrosion while specifying protective measures for long-term durability.

### Earthquake Damage

Seismic events cause foundation damage requiring professional assessment and repair.

**Seismic Damage Types:**
- Shear cracking
- Foundation-to-structure connection failure
- Liquefaction-induced settlement
- Lateral spreading damage
- Anchor bolt pullout

Our engineers evaluate seismic damage and design repairs that restore and improve structural performance.

### Age-Related Deterioration

**Aging Foundation Issues:**
- Concrete degradation
- Inadequate reinforcement
- Outdated construction methods
- Code-deficient conditions
- Deferred maintenance effects

We design repairs that address deterioration while bringing properties closer to current standards.

## Foundation Repair Solutions

### Underpinning Systems

Underpinning extends foundations to stable bearing and corrects settlement.

**Underpinning Options:**
- Push pier systems
- Helical pile installation
- Micropile underpinning
- Jet grouting stabilization
- Concrete pier additions

Our engineers select underpinning methods appropriate for specific soil conditions and access constraints.

### Crack Repair Engineering

Foundation crack repair ranges from simple sealing to structural reinforcement.

**Crack Repair Methods:**
- Epoxy injection for structural cracks

- Carbon fiber reinforcement
- Steel stitching repairs
- Section rebuilding
