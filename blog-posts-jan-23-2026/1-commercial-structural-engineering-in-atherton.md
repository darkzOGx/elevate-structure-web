## What is Commercial Structural Engineering?

Commercial structural engineering encompasses the design, analysis, and certification of structural systems for non-residential buildings including office complexes, retail centers, mixed-use developments, medical facilities, and industrial properties. Unlike residential engineering, commercial projects involve larger spans, heavier loads, more complex building systems, and stringent code requirements that demand specialized PE expertise.

In Atherton and the surrounding Silicon Valley communities, commercial structural engineering takes on additional complexity due to the region's seismic activity, high property values, and sophisticated client expectations. Our commercial structural engineers analyze building loads, design structural framing systems, specify connections and foundations, and produce PE-stamped construction documents that satisfy San Mateo County building department requirements.

Commercial structural engineering services in Atherton include new construction design, tenant improvement engineering, seismic retrofit analysis, structural assessments for property transactions, and adaptive reuse projects that transform existing structures for new commercial purposes.
