## What is Tenant Improvement Engineering?

Tenant improvement engineering encompasses structural analysis and design services required when commercial tenants modify leased spaces to suit their business operations. These modifications frequently involve structural elements that require California PE-licensed engineering to ensure safety, code compliance, and permit approval.

In Coronado's compact commercial market, tenant improvement projects range from boutique retail buildouts along Orange Avenue to restaurant renovations in the historic district and professional office configurations in newer developments. Our structural engineers evaluate existing conditions, analyze proposed modifications, design required structural elements, and produce PE-stamped construction documents that satisfy City of Coronado building department requirements.

Tenant improvement engineering services include load-bearing wall removal, mezzanine additions, storefront modifications, heavy equipment support, seismic bracing, and structural assessments that inform lease negotiations and buildout planning.
