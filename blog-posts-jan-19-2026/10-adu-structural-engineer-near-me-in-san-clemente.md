**Can I build an ADU on a hillside lot in San Clemente?**
Yes, hillside ADUs are possible with proper engineering. Hillside construction requires specialized foundation design‚Äîoften drilled piers or stepped foundations‚Äîto accommodate slope conditions. **What size ADU can I build in San Clemente?**
California state law allows detached ADUs up to 1,200 square feet regardless of lot size. Attached ADUs can be up to 50% of existing home size or 1,200 square feet, whichever is less. San Clemente's specific requirements should be verified with the building department for your property.

**How long does ADU permit approval take in San Clemente?**
California law requires cities to approve ADU permits within 60 days of complete application submittal. San Clemente typically processes ADU permits within this timeframe. Incomplete applications or corrections extend the timeline. Well-prepared engineering documents help achieve faster approval.

**Can I convert my garage to an ADU?**
Yes, garage conversions are a popular ADU type. California law specifically protects the right to convert garages to ADUs. Structural engineering evaluates the existing garage and designs any necessary modifications. Replacement parking is generally not required for garage conversion ADUs under state law.
