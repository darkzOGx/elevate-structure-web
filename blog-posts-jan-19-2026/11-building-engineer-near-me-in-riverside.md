A building engineer (structural engineer) designs the structural systems that make buildings safe and stable. This includes foundations, floor systems, walls, columns, roof structures, and lateral force-resisting systems. Engineers ensure buildings safely support intended loads and resist earthquakes, wind, and other forces.
