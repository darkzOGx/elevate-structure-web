**How do I know if a wall is load-bearing?**
Load-bearing walls support structure above them and transfer loads to the foundation. They typically run perpendicular to floor/ceiling joists, are located below other walls, and may have posts in the basement below. A structural engineer provides definitive determination through analysis of your specific home's structure.

**Can any wall be removed?**
Most walls can be removed if proper structural support is provided to replace the wall's function. Load-bearing walls require beams and posts to carry transferred loads. Some walls may be impractical to remove due to very high loads or inadequate support options. Engineering evaluation determines feasibility and requirements.

**What permits do I need for remodel work in Redondo Beach?**
Redondo Beach requires building permits for structural modifications including wall removals, additions, and changes to the building envelope. Structural plans stamped by a licensed Professional Engineer are required for structural work. Your contractor or we can help navigate the permit process.
