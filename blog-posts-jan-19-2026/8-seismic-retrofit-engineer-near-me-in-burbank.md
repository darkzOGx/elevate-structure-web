**What is foundation bolting?**
Foundation bolting secures wood-frame buildings to their concrete foundations using anchor bolts and steel hardware. Many older homes simply rest on foundations without positive connection. Earthquakes can cause unbolted houses to slide off foundations. Foundation bolting prevents this failure mode.

**What is a soft-story building?**
A soft-story building has a weak ground floor‚Äîtypically due to tuck-under parking, large storefronts, or open areas‚Äîwhile upper floors have more walls. The weak story can collapse in earthquakes, as seen in the Northridge earthquake. Soft-story retrofits add strength to vulnerable ground floors.
