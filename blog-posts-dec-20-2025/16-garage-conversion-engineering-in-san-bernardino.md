## What Are Common Garage Conversion Challenges in San Bernardino?

Common San Bernardino garage conversion challenges include low ceiling heights, inadequate foundations, lack of natural light, and coordination with existing utility services. Our engineers develop solutions for each site's specific conditions.

**Challenge 1: Low Ceiling Height**

**Problem:** Many garages have 7-8 foot ceilings, below the 7'6" code minimum for habitable space.

**Solution:** Raised floor with stepped entry, or assess feasibility of raising roof structure.

**Challenge 2: Foundation Issues**

**Problem:** Garage slabs may be too thin, cracked, or lacking proper moisture protection.

**San Bernardino Solution:**
- Overlay with new concrete if needed
- Install vapor barrier and insulation
- Add raised floor system for grade issues

**Challenge 3: Heating/Cooling**

**Problem:** San Bernardino's hot summers require adequate HVAC for converted spaces.

**Solution:** Design accommodates ductwork, mini-splits, or other HVAC solutions with proper structural support.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can any garage be converted in San Bernardino?**
Most garages can be converted with appropriate engineering. Size, condition, and local regulations determine feasibility and cost.

**Do I lose my parking requirement if I convert my garage?**
California ADU law has eliminated parking requirements for ADU garage conversions in most cases. Check current San Bernardino requirements for your specific situation.

**How long does garage conversion construction take?**
San Bernardino garage conversions typically take 2-4 months from permit to completion, depending on scope and contractor availability.

**Is a garage conversion a good investment in San Bernardino?**
With rental rates of $1,500-$2,500/month for San Bernardino ADUs, conversions typically pay for themselves within 5-8 years while adding property value.

**Do I need to replace the garage door with a wall?**
Yes, for habitable space the garage door must be replaced with a code-compliant wall assembly including proper framing, insulation, and exterior finish.

**Can I keep part of my garage as storage?**
Yes, partial conversions are possible. We can design conversions that maintain some garage/storage space while adding living area.
