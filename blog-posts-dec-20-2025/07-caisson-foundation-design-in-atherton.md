## Frequently Asked Questions

**How deep do caissons typically go in Atherton?**
Atherton caisson depths range from 20-50+ feet depending on location and geotechnical conditions. Eastern Atherton near Highway 280 generally requires deeper caissons than western areas.

**What diameter caissons are used for Atherton estates?**
Residential caissons typically range from 24"-48" diameter, with larger diameters for heavy point loads or deep basement construction. Specific sizing depends on structural loads and soil conditions.

**How long does caisson construction take?**
Caisson drilling typically takes 2-4 weeks for residential projects, depending on soil conditions and caisson count. Larger estates may require longer installation periods.

**Do I need a geotechnical report for Atherton construction?**
Yes, the Town of Atherton requires geotechnical investigation for new construction. The report guides caisson design and satisfies permit requirements.

**Can caissons be installed in wet conditions?**
Yes, caissons can be installed through groundwater using casing and tremie concrete placement techniques. These conditions require additional engineering consideration.

**How do caissons connect to the superstructure?**
Grade beams span between caissons, creating a rigid foundation framework. The building structure connects to grade beams through anchor bolts and reinforcement ties.
