## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is my Santa Monica building subject to the retrofit ordinance?**
Buildings with 3+ stories and ground-floor parking or commercial space, built before 1978, are typically subject. Check with Santa Monica Building & Safety for official determination.

**What happens if I miss my retrofit deadline?**
Non-compliant buildings face daily fines, rent restrictions, and may have difficulty selling or refinancing. Compliance is mandatory.

**How long does retrofit construction take?**
Santa Monica soft-story retrofits typically take 3-6 months for construction, depending on building size and retrofit scope.

**Can tenants stay during construction?**
Usually yes. We design retrofits minimizing tenant disruption, with most work in parking and common areas.

**Are there financing options for retrofits?**
Yes, PACE financing and other programs may be available. Some costs can be passed to tenants per local regulations.

**How do I find a soft-story retrofit engineer near me in Santa Monica?**
Look for a California-licensed PE with Santa Monica retrofit experience. Verify their license at bpelsg.ca.gov and ask for local references.
