## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is MEP engineering?**
MEP stands for Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing‚Äîthe building systems that provide climate control, power, lighting, water, and drainage. MEP engineering designs these systems for code compliance and efficient operation.

**Do I need MEP engineering for my Anaheim project?**
Commercial buildings typically require MEP engineering. Residential projects may need MEP design for custom homes, major renovations, or ADUs with complex systems.

**How does Title 24 affect MEP design?**
Title 24 sets energy efficiency requirements affecting HVAC system selection, lighting design, water heating, and building envelope. Compliance documentation is required for permits.

**Can you design just one discipline (M, E, or P)?**
Yes, we can provide individual discipline services or complete MEP packages depending on project needs.

**How do MEP systems coordinate with structural and architectural design?**
MEP design must coordinate with structure (for equipment support, penetrations) and architecture (for ceiling heights, mechanical rooms). We work with your design team for seamless coordination.

**What utilities serve Anaheim buildings?**
Southern California Edison provides electricity, Southern California Gas provides natural gas, and Anaheim Public Utilities provides water and sewer service in parts of the city.
