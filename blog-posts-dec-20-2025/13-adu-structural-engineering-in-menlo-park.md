## What ADU Types Require Structural Engineering in Menlo Park?

All ADU types in Menlo Park require structural engineering including detached ADUs, garage conversions, attached additions, and junior ADUs. Our licensed Professional Engineers provide complete structural design for each ADU type, addressing specific challenges of each configuration.

**Detached ADUs**

**New Construction on Your Property**
Detached ADUs require complete structural design:
- Foundation system design
- Wall framing and lateral resistance
- Roof structure
- Connection details

**Menlo Park Considerations:**
Detached ADUs must comply with setback requirements and height limits while maximizing usable space on Menlo Park's typically modest lot sizes.

**Garage Conversions**

**Transforming Existing Structures**
Converting garages to living space requires:
- Foundation evaluation/upgrade
- Front wall replacement (former door opening)
- Egress window installation
- Lateral bracing modifications

**Common Menlo Park Challenge:**
Many Menlo Park garages have inadequate foundations for living space loads, requiring foundation enhancement.

**Attached ADUs**

**Additions to Existing Homes**
Attached ADUs require connection to existing structure:
- Integration with existing foundation
- Match existing roof lines
- Wall connection detailing
- Seismic load path continuity

**Junior ADUs (JADUs)**

**Within Existing Living Space**
JADUs up to 500 SF require:
- Code compliance verification
- Egress requirements
- Fire separation if applicable
- Minimal structural modifications typically

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is the maximum ADU size in Menlo Park?**
Menlo Park allows ADUs up to 1,200 SF for detached units, with attached ADU size depending on lot size and existing home. JADUs are limited to 500 SF.

**Do I need a separate utility connection for my Menlo Park ADU?**
Not always. ADUs can share utilities with the main home in many cases, though separate meters may be required or preferred.

**Can I build an ADU on any Menlo Park property?**
Most residential properties can accommodate ADUs under California state law, but specific requirements vary by lot size and zoning.

**How long does ADU permitting take in Menlo Park?**
Menlo Park typically reviews ADU permits within 30-60 days when plans are complete and compliant.

**Is a garage conversion cheaper than a detached ADU?**
Generally yes, but foundation upgrades and reconfiguration can narrow the gap. Total costs depend on existing garage condition.

**Can I rent my Menlo Park ADU on Airbnb?**
Short-term rental regulations vary. Check current Menlo Park ordinances regarding ADU rental restrictions.
