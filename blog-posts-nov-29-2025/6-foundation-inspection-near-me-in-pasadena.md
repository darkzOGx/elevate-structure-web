## Frequently Asked Questions: Foundation Inspections in Pasadena

### How much does a foundation inspection cost in Pasadena?

Foundation inspections in Pasadena typically cost $500-$1,500 depending on property size and complexity. Historic homes and hillside properties are at the higher end. Pre-purchase inspections with detailed reports run $600-$1,200.

### What causes foundation problems in Pasadena homes?

Common causes include: (1) Expansive clay soils that swell/shrink with moisture, (2) Age—many Pasadena homes have 80-100+ year old foundations, (3) Hillside slope movement, (4) Inadequate drainage directing water toward foundations, (5) Tree roots affecting soil moisture, (6) Seismic activity over time.

### Should I get a foundation inspection for a historic Pasadena home?

Strongly recommended. Pasadena's Craftsman, Victorian, and Spanish Colonial homes often have foundations that predate modern standards. A PE foundation inspection identifies issues like unreinforced masonry, settlement, and seismic vulnerabilities—essential information before purchase or major renovation.
