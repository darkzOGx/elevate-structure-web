## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do I need a structural engineer for my home remodel?**
Any project involving load-bearing walls, foundation work, or structural modifications requires engineering. Cosmetic changes typically don't. We can help determine if your project needs engineering.

**How do I know if a wall is load-bearing?**
Professional evaluation is the only reliable way to determine if a wall is load-bearing. General indicators include walls running perpendicular to floor joists and walls stacked on multiple floors.

**How long does residential structural engineering take?**
Most home projects take 1-3 weeks for engineering, plus San Diego County permit review time (typically 2-4 weeks for residential).
