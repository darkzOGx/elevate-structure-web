## Frequently Asked Questions

**How long does ADU structural engineering take?**
Most ADU engineering takes 2-3 weeks, plus Riverside County permit review (typically 4-8 weeks for ADUs).

**Do I need an architect and a structural engineer for my ADU?**
Most ADUs require both architectural plans (layout, finishes, code compliance) and structural engineering (foundation, framing, connections). Some engineers and architects offer combined services.

**Can I build an ADU if I have an HOA?**
California law limits HOA restrictions on ADUs, but some requirements may apply. Check with your HOA and consider consulting an attorney if issues arise.

**What's the maximum ADU size allowed in Palm Desert?**
State law allows up to 1,200 SF for detached ADUs. Attached ADUs may be limited based on primary residence size. Local rules may vary.

**How much does it cost to build an ADU in Palm Desert?**
Complete ADU construction typically costs $200-$400 per square foot, depending on finishes and site conditions. Engineering is a small portion of overall project cost.
