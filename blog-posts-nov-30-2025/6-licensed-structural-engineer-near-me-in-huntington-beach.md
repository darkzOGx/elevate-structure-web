## How Do You Verify a Structural Engineer's License in California?

**Direct Answer:**
Verify any California structural engineer's license status at the Board for Professional Engineers, Land Surveyors, and Geologists website (bpelsg.ca.gov). Enter the engineer's name or license number to confirm active status, license type, expiration date, and any disciplinary actions. This free verification takes 30 seconds and protects you from unlicensed practitioners.

**Verification Steps:**

1. **Visit bpelsg.ca.gov** - California licensing board website
2. **Click "License Verification"** - Public lookup tool
3. **Enter engineer's name** - Or license number if provided
4. **Review results** - Check for:
   - License type (Civil or Structural)
   - Active status
   - Expiration date
   - Disciplinary actions

**What to Look For:**

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Active | Currently licensed and in good standing |
| Expired | License lapsed - cannot stamp plans |
| Inactive | Voluntarily inactive - cannot practice |
| Revoked | License removed for cause |
| Suspended | Temporarily cannot practice |

**Red Flags:**
- Unable to provide license number
- License not verifiable online
- Disciplinary actions on record
- Expired or inactive status
