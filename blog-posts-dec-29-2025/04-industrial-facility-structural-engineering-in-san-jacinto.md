## What Types of Industrial Structures Are Common in San Jacinto?

**Direct Answer:** San Jacinto industrial facilities include steel frame manufacturing plants, concrete tilt-up processing facilities, and pre-engineered metal buildings for light industrial operations. Each structural system suits different operational requirements and equipment configurations.

**Concrete Tilt-Up Processing Facilities**

**Pre-Engineered Metal Buildings**
