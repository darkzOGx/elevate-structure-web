## What Makes Desert Foundation Engineering Different?

Desert environments present unique challenges including extreme temperature cycling with summer highs over 115°F, daily temperature swings of 30-40°F, sandy soils, caliche layers, expansive clays, and moisture extremes.

## Foundation Types for Cathedral City

### Post-Tensioned Slab

The preferred solution for most desert sites with cable tendons in both directions providing crack resistance and flatness.

### Standard Slab-on-Grade

For favorable soil conditions with minimum 4" slab thickness, thickened edges, steel reinforcement, and vapor barrier.
