## Seismic Retrofit Services

### Foundation Bolting

Securing homes to foundations:

- Anchor bolt installation
- Sill plate repair/replacement
- Foundation crack repair
- Connection hardware

**Why It Matters**: Without proper bolting, homes can slide off foundations during earthquakes.

### Cripple Wall Bracing

Strengthening short walls between foundation and floor:

- Structural plywood installation
- Proper nailing patterns
- Hardware connections
- Ventilation maintenance

**Why It Matters**: Cripple walls are the most common failure point in older homes.

### Soft-Story Retrofit

Strengthening garage and large opening areas:

- Steel moment frames
- Plywood shear walls
- Foundation strengthening
- Connection upgrades
