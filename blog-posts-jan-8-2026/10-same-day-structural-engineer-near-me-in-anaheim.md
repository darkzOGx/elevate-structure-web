## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I get same-day structural engineering on weekends?**

Weekend availability is limited but possible for urgent situations with additional premium (typically 50-100% over standard rates). Call early in the day for best availability. True emergencies (safety concerns, imminent deadlines) receive priority. For non-urgent matters, scheduling for Monday often provides better availability and lower cost.

**What if I need full structural drawings same day?**

Full structural drawing packages typically cannot be completed same-day due to the calculation, drafting, and review time required. However, we can begin the process same-day with site assessment and scope development, then expedite the design phase with rush timelines (additional fees apply). For some simple conditions, preliminary sketches may be possible same-day.

**How do I know if my situation qualifies for same-day service?**

Call and describe your situation‚Äîwe'll quickly assess whether same-day service is appropriate and available. Generally, visual assessments, consultations, and letter reports can be same-day. Complex analysis, extensive calculations, and full drawing packages require more time. The key factors are: scope simplicity, timeline criticality, and our current schedule availability.

**What's the difference between same-day and emergency service?**

Emergency service is for life-safety situations‚Äîbuilding collapse, major earthquake damage, unsafe conditions requiring immediate response. Same-day service is for urgent but non-emergency situations‚Äîbusiness deadlines, permit issues, real estate transactions. Emergency service commands highest priority and premium; same-day service is scheduled around current commitments when possible.

**Will a same-day assessment be thorough enough?**

Same-day assessments are professionally thorough for their intended purpose‚Äîanswering specific questions based on visual observation and professional judgment. They may identify needs for additional investigation (destructive testing, detailed analysis) that require more time. We clearly communicate what can be determined same-day and what requires further work.

**Can I use a same-day letter for permit purposes?**

Depending on the situation, yes. Simple letters confirming existing conditions or providing professional opinion are often sufficient. Complex permit requirements (new designs, retrofits) typically need full engineering documents that take more time. We'll advise whether same-day deliverables will meet your permit needs or whether expedited full engineering is required.
