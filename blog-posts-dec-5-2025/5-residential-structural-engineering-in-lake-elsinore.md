## What Residential Projects Require Structural Engineering in California?

**Direct Answer:**
California requires structural engineering for projects that modify load-bearing elements, add significant floor area, involve foundation work, or change a building's structural configuration.

**Home Additions**
Adding rooms, expanding kitchens, or extending living spaces requires structural engineering for foundations, framing, and connections.

**Second-Story Additions**
Adding floors above existing construction significantly increases loads, often requiring foundation upgrades.

**Load-Bearing Wall Removal**
Creating open floor plans by removing walls requires engineered beams and supporting posts.

**New Home Construction**
Custom homes require complete structural engineering from foundation to roof.
