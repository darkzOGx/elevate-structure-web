## What Types of Foundation Systems Are Used in Spring Valley?

**Direct Answer:**
Spring Valley projects commonly use spread footings, continuous footings, grade beam and pier systems, and post-tensioned slabs. The optimal foundation type depends on soil conditions, building loads, site topography, and project requirements.

**Spread Footings**
Individual footings under columns and posts that spread loads to underlying soil. Common for light to moderate loads on stable soils.

**Continuous Footings**
Linear footings under bearing walls that distribute loads along their length. Standard for conventional residential construction.

**Grade Beams**
Reinforced concrete beams spanning between supports that resist both gravity and lateral loads.

**Post-Tensioned Slabs**
Concrete slabs with tensioned steel tendons that resist cracking and settlement. Effective for expansive soil conditions.
