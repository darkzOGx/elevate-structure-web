## What Types of ADUs Are Common in Perris?

**Direct Answer:**
Perris property owners typically build detached backyard ADUs, garage conversions, or attached ADUs. Each type has distinct structural requirements.

**Detached ADUs**

**Garage Conversions**
Converting existing garages to living space requires structural analysis of existing construction and potential foundation modifications.

**Attached ADUs**
Units connected to the primary residence requiring proper connection between new and existing construction.
