## Frequently Asked Questions

### How deep do foundations go in Atherton?

Foundation depth depends on location and soil conditions. Eastern Atherton near the Bay may require piers 50-70+ feet to reach competent bearing. Central and western areas typically require 25-45 feet. Geotechnical investigation determines exact depth requirements for your property.

### Do I need a geotechnical report before foundation design?

Yes, Town of Atherton requires geotechnical investigation for all deep foundation projects. The geotech report provides soil data, bearing recommendations, and groundwater information essential for design. We can recommend qualified Peninsula geotechnical engineers.

### Can you add a basement to an existing Atherton home?

Yes, with proper engineering. Basement additions require underpinning the existing foundation using helical piles or new drilled piers. The process involves careful excavation sequencing to maintain existing structure support. We've completed numerous Atherton basement additions.

### How long does deep foundation design take?

Engineering design typically takes 4-6 weeks from geotechnical report review through permit-ready plans. Town of Atherton plan check adds 2-4 weeks. Rush schedules are available for time-sensitive projects.
