## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I know if my building needs seismic retrofit during renovation?**
California Building Code triggers apply when renovation costs exceed thresholds or when making certain changes. We provide code analysis early in project planning to identify requirements before design.

**Can buildings be modernized while tenants remain?**
Yes, phased construction planning allows modernization while maintaining building operations. Engineering addresses construction sequencing and temporary conditions.

**What structural modifications enable open floor plans?**
Removing walls requires alternative structural support—new beams, columns, or moment frames. Engineering determines appropriate modifications based on existing structure and desired layout.
