## Frequently Asked Questions

**What structural system is most cost-effective for small commercial buildings?**
Wood frame construction typically offers the lowest cost for smaller buildings under 5,000 square feet. Steel frame or masonry may be more economical for larger buildings or where fire rating requirements apply.

**Do small commercial buildings need the same engineering as large ones?**
All commercial buildings require PE-stamped structural plans, but engineering scope scales with building complexity. Small buildings can use simpler, prescriptive solutions where code allows.

**How can structural engineering save money on small commercial projects?**
Efficient design minimizes materials and construction complexity. Right-sized foundations, appropriate structural systems, and buildable details all reduce construction cost while meeting code requirements.
