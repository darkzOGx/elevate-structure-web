## Frequently Asked Questions

**How are anchor tenant requirements coordinated with in-line retail?**
We coordinate with anchor tenant engineers to establish interface requirements while independently designing in-line portions. Clear tenant criteria ensure in-line tenants understand their modification limits.

**What structural provisions are needed for restaurant tenants?**
Restaurants require enhanced floor capacity for equipment, provisions for grease interceptors, rooftop exhaust equipment support, and potentially enhanced outdoor dining structures.

**What systems are typical for shopping center construction?**
Concrete tilt-up is common for efficiency and durability. Steel frame and wood construction serve smaller or specialized buildings. System selection considers building size, tenant mix, and budget.
