## Frequently Asked Questions

**What makes marina commercial structures different from standard commercial?**
Marina structures face continuous marine environment exposure requiring corrosion-resistant materials, protective coatings, and detailing that prevents salt infiltration. Foundation design addresses coastal soils and potential wave action.

**How does Coastal Commission permitting affect structural engineering?**
Coastal Commission review evaluates structural design for visual impact, height compliance, and compatibility with harbor character. Engineering must balance performance requirements with aesthetic and regulatory constraints.

**What materials are best for marina commercial construction?**
Stainless steel connections, galvanized or epoxy-coated reinforcement, marine-grade concrete, and treated or naturally durable wood are typical. Material selection balances durability, cost, and appearance.
