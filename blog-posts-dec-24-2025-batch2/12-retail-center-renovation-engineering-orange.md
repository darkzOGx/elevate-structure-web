## Frequently Asked Questions

**When does retail renovation trigger seismic upgrade requirements?**
California Building Code generally requires seismic evaluation when renovation costs exceed 50% of building replacement value, or when adding square footage, changing occupancy, or creating structural irregularities. Early code analysis identifies requirements before design proceeds.

**How long does retail renovation engineering take?**
Typical projects require 4-8 weeks from assessment to permit documents. Projects requiring seismic analysis may require additional time. Targeted modifications like tenant improvements may be completed faster.

**What if original construction drawings aren't available?**
Field investigation documents existing conditions including framing sizes, connections, and foundation systems. This investigation adds cost but ensures modifications are properly designed and safely constructed.
