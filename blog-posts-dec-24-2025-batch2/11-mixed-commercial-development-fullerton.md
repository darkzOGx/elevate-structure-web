## Frequently Asked Questions

**How long does mixed-use structural engineering take in Fullerton?**
Mixed-use projects typically require 6-12 weeks from design initiation to permit-ready documents due to complexity of coordinating multiple structural systems and occupancy types.

**What is podium construction and why is it used for mixed-use?**
Podium construction features a concrete structure (typically for parking and retail) with lighter wood or steel framing above (for residential). This approach optimizes each use—durable, fire-resistant concrete for commercial while cost-effective wood framing provides residential.

**What are transfer structures in mixed-use buildings?**
Transfer structures redistribute loads where column grids change between uses. Retail may require wide column spacing for flexibility while residential above has more frequent bearing walls. Transfer beams and columns bridge between these different patterns.

**How does seismic design work for mixed construction types?**
Buildings with different construction types (concrete below, wood above) require careful seismic analysis addressing how forces transfer through the structure. California Building Code provides requirements for these "vertical combinations."
