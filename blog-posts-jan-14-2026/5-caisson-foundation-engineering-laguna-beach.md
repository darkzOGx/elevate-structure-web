## What is Caisson Foundation Engineering?

Caisson foundation engineering involves the design of deep drilled pier foundations that transfer building loads through weak or unstable surface soils to competent bearing strata below. Our California PE-licensed structural engineers design caisson foundations for Laguna Beach hillside homes, coastal properties, and structures on challenging terrain throughout Orange County.

Laguna Beach's dramatic topography—steep canyons, coastal bluffs, and hillside lots—makes caisson foundations essential for many residential and commercial projects. With median home values exceeding $3.2 million, Laguna Beach property owners invest in premium foundation solutions that ensure long-term structural stability.

**Quick Answer:** Caisson foundation engineering in Laguna Beach typically costs $15,000-$50,000 for structural design, with construction costs of $2,500-$6,000 per caisson. Most hillside homes require 6-20 caissons depending on size and site conditions.

## Why Laguna Beach Properties Need Caisson Foundations

### Challenging Site Conditions

Laguna Beach presents unique foundation challenges including steep terrain, variable soil conditions, landslide hazards, coastal bluff erosion, expansive soils, and high groundwater.
