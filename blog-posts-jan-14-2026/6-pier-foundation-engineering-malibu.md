## What is Pier Foundation Engineering?

Pier foundation engineering involves the design of deep foundation elements that transfer structural loads through unstable surface soils to stable bearing strata below. Our California PE-licensed structural engineers design pier foundations for Malibu's unique coastal and canyon terrain, ensuring long-term stability for properties valued at $4.5 million and above.

Malibu's 21-mile coastline and rugged Santa Monica Mountains create some of Southern California's most challenging foundation conditions. Between active landslides, coastal erosion, wildfire zones, and seismic hazards, Malibu properties require specialized foundation engineering to protect significant investments.

**Quick Answer:** Pier foundation engineering in Malibu typically costs $20,000-$75,000 for structural design, with construction costs of $12,000-$35,000 per pier. Malibu homes commonly require 10-30+ piers depending on site conditions.
