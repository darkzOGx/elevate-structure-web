## Specialized Manufacturing Considerations

### Crane System Engineering

Overhead crane systems require specialized structural design including runway beam design, crane column design, bracket connections, foundation loads, deflection limits, and fatigue design.

### Clean Room and Controlled Environment

Some manufacturing requires controlled environments including structural isolation, specialized foundations, and precise levelness.
