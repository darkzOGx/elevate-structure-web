## Foundation Repair Strategies

**Foundation Underpinning**
Underpinning extends existing foundations to deeper, more stable soil. Underpinning approaches include helical piers extending to load-bearing strata, push piers driven to refusal, or drilled caissons for hillside applications. We design underpinning systems appropriate for soil conditions and building loads.

**Crack Repair Methods**
Foundation crack repairs depend on crack severity and causes. Minor shrinkage cracks may require epoxy injection or sealant. Structural cracks need repair addressing underlying causes. We design appropriate repair strategies for observed conditions.

**Drainage Improvements**
Many foundation issues stem from inadequate drainage. Foundation repairs often include drainage improvements with subdrain installation, downspout extensions, site regrading, or waterproofing systems. Proper drainage prevents future foundation damage.

## Hillside Foundation Design

**Caisson Foundation Systems**
Steep Laguna Beach hillside sites typically require caisson foundations with drilled piers extending to competent soil or bedrock. Caissons support building loads while resisting slope movement. We design caisson systems with appropriate depths, diameters, and reinforcement for hillside conditions.

Grade beams connect caissons creating integrated foundation systems. Caisson and grade beam design addresses seismic forces, slope stability, and building loads.

**Retaining Wall Design**
Hillside development requires retaining walls creating level building pads and supporting slopes. Retaining wall types include cantilevered concrete walls, crib walls with geogrid reinforcement, or tie-back walls for tall heights. We design retaining systems appropriate for site conditions.

Retaining wall drainage prevents hydrostatic pressure with subdrain systems and weep holes. Proper drainage ensures wall stability and long-term performance.

**Slope Stabilization**
Unstable slopes may require stabilization before foundation construction. Stabilization approaches include soil nailing, tie-back systems, or soil reinforcement. Geotechnical engineers and structural engineers collaborate on slope stabilization designs.
