## Frequently Asked Questions

### How long does Beverly Hills commercial renovation permit review take?
Standard plan check takes 3-4 weeks. Projects requiring Design Review add 4-6 weeks. Historic properties may add 6-8 weeks. Expedited review is available for additional fees, reducing standard review to 1-2 weeks.

### Do tenant improvements in Beverly Hills require structural engineering?
Any modification affecting load-bearing elements, creating floor openings, adding significant equipment loads, or altering the building's structural system requires PE-stamped structural drawings. Minor non-structural improvements may not require structural engineering.

### What triggers seismic upgrade requirements for Beverly Hills commercial renovation?
Seismic upgrade is required when alteration costs exceed 50% of building replacement value, occupancy classification changes to a higher risk category, or substantial structural alterations are proposed. Our engineers evaluate trigger thresholds during initial project assessment.

### Can historic commercial buildings in Beverly Hills be renovated?
Yes, with proper planning and Historic Preservation Commission approval. Our engineers specialize in historic structure modifications that meet both structural safety requirements and preservation standards.

### How do you coordinate structural engineering with interior design in luxury commercial projects?
We collaborate early with design teams to identify structural constraints and opportunities. Our solutions integrate aesthetic requirements with structural necessity, including concealed connections, minimized visible structure, and creative load path development.
