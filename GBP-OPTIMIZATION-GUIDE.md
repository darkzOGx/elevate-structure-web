## Performance Tracking

### Google Business Profile Insights

**Key Metrics to Track Monthly:**

#### Discovery Metrics
- **Total Views:** How many people saw your listing
  - Goal: 10% month-over-month growth
  - Benchmark: 500+ views/month in first 3 months

- **Search Views:** How people found you
  - Direct searches (searched your business name)
  - Discovery searches (searched category/service)
  - Goal: 60% discovery, 40% direct

#### Engagement Metrics
- **Website Clicks:** Clicks to your website from GBP
  - Goal: 5-8% click-through rate
  - Benchmark: 25+ clicks/month

- **Phone Calls:** Calls from "Call" button
  - Goal: 3-5% of views convert to calls
  - Benchmark: 15+ calls/month

- **Direction Requests:** Clicks on "Directions"
  - Goal: 10+ requests/month
  - Shows strong local intent

- **Photo Views:** Total photo views
  - Goal: 100+ views/month

   - Suspensions can be appealed
   - Most are resolved within 2-4 weeks
