## Warning Signs You Need Foundation Assessment in Oceanside

Contact a foundation engineer immediately if you notice:

**Interior Warning Signs:**
- Cracks in drywall, especially diagonal cracks near doors and windows
- Doors that stick or won't close properly
- Uneven or sloping floors
- Gaps between walls and ceiling
- Cracked tile or buckling flooring

**Exterior Warning Signs:**
- Visible cracks in foundation walls
- Gaps between foundation and siding
- Chimney leaning or separating from house
- Concrete steps pulling away from foundation
- Standing water near foundation

**Urgent Signs Requiring Immediate Assessment:**
- Horizontal cracks in foundation walls
- Bowing or bulging foundation walls
- Large cracks (wider than 1/4 inch)
- Evidence of foundation sinking or settlement

## Common Foundation Issues We Find in Oceanside

Based on our extensive experience in San Diego County, common foundation issues in Oceanside include:

### Settlement Issues
Uneven settlement occurs when different parts of the foundation move at different rates. Oceanside's varying soil conditions—from sandy coastal areas to clay-rich inland zones—contribute to differential settlement.

### Concrete Deterioration
Salt air exposure in coastal Oceanside can accelerate concrete deterioration through carbonation and chloride intrusion, weakening the foundation over time.

### Drainage Problems
Inadequate drainage causes water to pool near foundations, leading to soil expansion and erosion. Many Oceanside properties require improved drainage systems to protect foundations.

### Earthquake Damage
Even minor seismic events can cause hairline cracks that worsen over time. Post-earthquake assessments are essential for Oceanside homes.
