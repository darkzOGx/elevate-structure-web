## What Is Structural Inspection?

Structural inspection is a comprehensive evaluation of a building's load-bearing components by a qualified professional. Unlike general home inspection, structural inspection focuses specifically on:

- Foundation condition
- Wall framing integrity
- Roof structure
- Floor system
- Load-bearing connections
- Signs of structural distress

In Villa Park, with its mix of custom homes and established neighborhoods, structural inspection helps protect property investments and ensure safety.

## What Structural Inspectors Check in Villa Park

### Foundation Assessment

**Exterior Inspection:**
- Visible foundation walls
- Cracks (pattern, width, direction)
- Settlement signs
- Drainage conditions

**Interior Signs:**
- Floor levelness
- Door/window operation
- Wall cracks
- Basement/crawl space

### Framing Evaluation

**Wall Framing:**
- Load-bearing wall identification
- Evidence of modification
- Moisture damage
- Pest damage

**Roof Structure:**
- Rafter/truss condition
- Connections and bracing
- Sagging or deflection
- Attic ventilation

**Floor System:**
- Joist condition
- Beam integrity
- Support columns
- Bounce or deflection

### Connection Review

**Critical Connections:**
- Foundation to framing
- Wall to roof
- Floor to wall
- Beam to column

### Damage Documentation

**Types of Damage:**
- Earthquake damage
- Water/moisture damage
- Termite/pest damage
- Fire damage
- Age-related deterioration
