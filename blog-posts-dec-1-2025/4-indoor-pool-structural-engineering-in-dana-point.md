## What Is Indoor Pool Structural Engineering in Dana Point?

Indoor pool structural engineering involves designing the complete structural system for enclosed swimming facilities, including the pool shell foundation, building structure (walls, roof, glazing), deck and surrounding areas, and integration with mechanical ventilation systems. In Dana Point, this includes addressing coastal soil conditions, corrosion resistance, and Coastal Commission requirements.

Indoor pools are among the most structurally demanding residential features due to the combination of heavy water loads, high humidity environments, chemical exposure, and building enclosure requirements.
