## What Are Citations?

**Citations** are online mentions of your business name, address, and phone number (NAP) on other websites. They are critical for local SEO because:

1. **Google uses citations to verify your business information**
2. **Consistent NAP across the web builds trust**
3. **Citations from authoritative sites improve local rankings**
4. **More citations = better visibility in local search and Google Maps**

## Citation Monitoring & Audits

### Monthly Citation Audit (30 minutes/month)

**First Week of Each Month:**

1. **Check Random 10 Citations:**
   - Select 10 random citations from your completed list
   - Verify NAP is still correct and unchanged
   - Check that listing is still live
   - Look for any unauthorized edits

2. **Search for Duplicate Listings:**
   - Google: `"AAA Engineering Design" "Stanton"`
   - Check for any duplicate listings on same platforms
   - Merge or delete duplicates immediately

3. **Monitor New Review Platforms:**
   - Check if reviews appeared on any citation platforms
   - Respond to reviews within 24 hours
   - Positive or negative, respond professionally

### Quarterly Deep Audit (2 hours/quarter)

**Every 3 Months:**

1. **Verify ALL Citations:**
   - Check every completed citation
   - Update any changed information
   - Fix any NAP inconsistencies found

2. **Competitive Analysis:**
   - Search for competitor citations
   - Identify citation opportunities you've missed
   - Prioritize high-value citations competitors have

3. **Remove Bad Citations:**
   - Look for low-quality spam directories
   - If citation is on sketchy site, request removal
   - Focus on quality over quantity

### Tools for Citation Monitoring

**Free Tools:**
- **Google Search:** `"AAA Engineering Design" "phone number"`
- **Google Alerts:** Set alert for your business name
- **Manual checking** of top 20-30 citations

**Paid Tools (Optional):**
- **Moz Local** ($129/year) - Citation monitoring and building

## Common Citation Mistakes to Avoid

### âŒ DON'T:

1. **Inconsistent NAP**
   - Using different phone numbers
   - Abbreviating "Street" differently
   - Variations in business name

2. **Duplicate Listings**
   - Creating multiple listings on same platform
   - Claiming AND creating new listing

3. **Keyword Stuffing Business Name**
   - âŒ "AAA Engineering Design - Structural Engineer Orange County"
   - âœ… "AAA Engineering Design"

4. **Using PO Boxes**
   - Citations need physical address
   - PO boxes hurt local SEO

5. **Fake/Spam Citations**
   - Low-quality foreign directories
   - Irrelevant industry directories
   - Sites that look spammy

6. **Setting Wrong Categories**
   - Choose most specific category available
   - Don't select unrelated categories for visibility

7. **Neglecting Ongoing Maintenance**
   - Citations need periodic updates
   - Check at least quarterly

8. **Paying for Citation Services Without Vetting**
   - Many citation "builders" use low-quality sites
   - Better to build manually or use reputable service

## Expert Tips

### Getting Maximum Value from Citations:

1. **Complete Profiles 100%**
   - Don't just add NAP and leave
   - Fill out every field
   - Add photos, descriptions, hours
   - More complete = more valuable

2. **Link to Specific Pages**
   - Don't always link to homepage
   - Link Houzz to portfolio/projects
   - Link industry sites to specific service pages
   - Better user experience = better SEO

3. **Consistent Branding**
   - Use same logo everywhere
   - Use same business description (or slight variations)
   - Same brand voice across platforms

4. **Get Reviews on Citation Sites**
   - Focus on Google and Yelp primarily
   - But don't ignore BBB, Facebook, Houzz reviews
   - Multi-platform review presence builds authority

5. **Leverage Citation Traffic**
   - Add UTM parameters to website URLs: `?utm_source=yelp&utm_medium=citation`
   - Track which citations send most traffic
   - Double-down on high-performing platforms

6. **Update Seasonally**
   - Refresh business description every 6 months
   - Add new photos quarterly
   - Update holiday hours in advance
   - Keep information fresh
