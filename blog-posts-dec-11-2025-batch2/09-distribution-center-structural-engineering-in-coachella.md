## What Structural Systems Support Distribution Centers?

Tilt-up is standard for distribution:

- Cost-effective for large buildings

- Local contractor expertise

### Structural Steel Framing

Steel offers advantages:

- Longer spans possible
- Lighter weight
- Future modification flexibility
- Faster erection

### Roof Systems

- Steel joists and joist girders
- Steel trusses
- Precast double tees

## What Are Critical Design Considerations?

**Direct Answer:** Critical considerations include designing long-span roof systems (100+ feet), heavy-duty floor slabs for rack point loads, steel storage rack anchorage per ANSI/RMI MH16.1, and efficient dock configurations. Engineers must also address Coachella's seismic requirements and desert climate conditions.

### Steel Storage Rack Anchorage

High-pile storage requires:

- Seismic rack design per ANSI/RMI MH16.1
- Floor anchorage calculations
- Baseplate design
- Aisle spacing for seismic drift

### Floor Slab Design

Distribution floors must support:

- High rack point loads (40,000+ lb per post)
- Heavy forklift traffic
- Joint-free areas for automation
- Flatness for automated systems

### Clear Height and Bay Spacing

Modern requirements:

- 36-40 foot clear heights standard
- 50-60 foot typical bay spacing
- Column-free racking aisles
- Dock approach clearances
