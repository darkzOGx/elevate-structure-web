## What Structural Systems Are Used for Lake Elsinore Retail Centers?

### Wood Frame Construction

Wood frame is common for smaller retail centers:

**Advantages:**
- Cost-effective construction
- Design flexibility
- Fast construction schedule
- Easy tenant modifications

**Applications:**
- Strip malls up to 15,000 SF
- Neighborhood retail
- Restaurant buildings
- Small multi-tenant buildings

### Masonry Construction

CMU (concrete masonry unit) construction offers:

**Benefits:**
- Fire resistance
- Sound isolation
- Durability
- Low maintenance

**Applications:**
- Anchor tenant spaces
- Buildings with party walls
- Fire-separated tenants

### Tilt-Up Concrete

Tilt-up is used for larger retail:

**Advantages:**
- Large format retail buildings
- Cost-effective for big boxes
- Excellent durability
- Fast construction
