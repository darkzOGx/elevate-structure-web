## What Are the ADU Requirements in Long Beach?

**Direct Answer:** Long Beach ADU requirements follow California state law with local implementation including maximum ADU size of 1,200 SF for detached units, no owner-occupancy requirement, relaxed setback requirements (4 feet for most ADUs), no parking requirement if within half-mile of transit, and streamlined 60-day permit review for qualifying projects.

**Size Limitations**

California and Long Beach permit:
- Detached ADU: Up to 1,200 SF
- Attached ADU: Up to 1,000 SF or 50% of primary home
- JADU: Up to 500 SF within existing structure
- Above-garage: Varies by lot coverage limits

**Setback Requirements**

ADU setback requirements in Long Beach:
- Rear setback: 4 feet minimum
- Side setback: 4 feet minimum
- Conversion of existing structure: No setback required
- Height: Generally limited to 16 feet for detached

**Permit Process**

Long Beach Development Services processes ADU permits:
- 60-day review timeline for compliant applications
- Ministerial approval (no discretionary review)
- Impact fees limited by state law
- School fees apply in certain districts
