## Safety & Structural Integrity

### Ensuring Building Safety

**Life Safety Protection**
Professional engineering design ensures your Glendale building:
- Supports all anticipated loads safely
- Resists seismic forces (critical in Seismic Zone 4)
- Withstands wind forces
- Maintains stability under all conditions
- Protects occupants from structural failure

**Seismic Safety**
Glendale's proximity to multiple fault lines (San Andreas, Sierra Madre, Verdugo) makes seismic design critical. Engineers:
- Calculate site-specific ground motion
- Design appropriate seismic force-resisting systems
- Detail connections for earthquake resistance
- Ensure non-structural components are properly braced
- Meet or exceed California Building Code requirements

**Long-Term Durability**
Proper engineering design ensures:
- Appropriate material selection for Glendale's climate
- Adequate structural capacity with safety factors
- Resistance to degradation over time
- Proper drainage and moisture protection
- Foundation systems suited to local soil conditions

### Preventing Structural Failures

**Avoiding Common Problems:**
- Foundation settlement or failure
- Sagging floors or roofs
- Cracked walls and ceilings
- Door and window misalignment
- Structural distress from inadequate design

**Real-World Example:**
A Glendale homeowner attempted a second-story addition without engineering. The existing foundation couldn't support the additional load, resulting in:

Professional engineering would have identified the issue during design, allowing proper foundation reinforcement at a fraction of the cost.
