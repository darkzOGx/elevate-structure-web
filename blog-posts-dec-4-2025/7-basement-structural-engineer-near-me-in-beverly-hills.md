### Shoring System Design

Protecting excavations and adjacent properties:

**Soldier Pile & Lagging:**
- Steel H-piles at 6-8 foot spacing
- Wood or concrete lagging
- Cost-effective for typical depths

**Secant Pile Walls:**
- Overlapping drilled shafts
- Better water control
- Required for deep excavations with groundwater

**Shotcrete/Soil Nail Walls:**
- Reinforced concrete over excavated face
- Appropriate for competent hillside soils

### Deep Foundation Systems

Supporting structures over subterranean spaces:

- **Mat Foundations**: Thick concrete slabs for basement floors
- **Caissons**: Drilled shafts for concentrated loads
- **Underpinning**: Protection of existing adjacent structures

### Waterproofing Engineering

Below-grade moisture protection:

- **Membrane Systems**: Continuous barriers against water intrusion
- **Drainage Systems**: Relief of hydrostatic pressure
- **Vapor Barriers**: Preventing moisture transmission
- **Sump Systems**: Active water management
