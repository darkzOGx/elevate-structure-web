## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I need an engineer for a deck in Desert Hot Springs?
Desert Hot Springs requires PE-stamped structural plans for decks over 30 inches high, attached to structures, or supporting significant loads (hot tubs, etc.). Simple low decks may not require engineering, but we can advise on your specific situation.

### How long does deck engineering take?
Most deck structural engineering in Desert Hot Springs takes 1-3 weeks from initial consultation to permit-ready plans depending on complexity.

### What materials work best for decks in the desert?
Desert climates require materials that resist extreme heat and UV exposure. We specify appropriate lumber grades, composite materials, and fasteners for the Desert Hot Springs climate in our engineering designs.

### Can you engineer patio covers attached to my house?
Yes, we engineer attached patio covers including ledger connections, roof structure, and foundations. Attached structures require engineering to ensure safe load transfer to your home.
